## 2. Mistake Selection — The Right 5

### Final 5 Mistakes (Research-Validated Order)

| Position | Mistake | Emotional Function | Serial Position |
|:--------:|---------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **#1** | "Balas 'siap kak' tanpa catat" | Agreement builder — most relatable | Primacy (remembered) |
| **#2** | "Catat di buku tulis / notes HP" | Escalation — deepening discomfort | Mid (supported by #1 agreement) |
| **#3** | "Chat WA pribadi campur pelanggan" | SHOCK — dead zone rescue | Dead zone (needs shock to compensate) |
| **#4** | "Gak tracking status pesanan" | Empathy cool-down — emotional weight | Mid (tonal variation prevents negativity overload) |
| **#5** | "Hitung omzet manual tiap malam" | Culmination — time + money + exhaustion | Recency (remembered, maximum urgency) |

### Why This Order

Based on three converging psychology frameworks:

1. **Serial Position Effect** (Ebbinghaus): Items at positions 1 and 5 are remembered best. Middle items (position 3) are forgotten most → deploy shock at position 3 to compensate.

2. **Peak-End Rule** (Kahneman): Experience is judged by two moments — the peak (most intense) and the end. Peak = Slide 4 (Mistake #3, the shock). End = Slide 7 (solution/relief).

3. **EPPM** (Extended Parallel Process Model): Fear appeals work ONLY when paired with efficacy. Five slides of threat → one slide of high-efficacy solution. If efficacy is missing, audience enters "fear control" (rejection/denial).

### The Emotional Arc — "W" Pattern

```
Emotional Intensity:

High  |           *SHOCK*                    *URGENCY*
      |          / (#3)  \                  / (#5)     \
Med   |    *AGREE*        *EMPATHY*        /    *RELIEF*
      |   / (#1)           (#4)  \       /     (solution)
Low   |  *CURIOSITY*               *---*
      |________________________________
       S1    S2    S3    S4    S5    S6    S7
       hook  #1    #2    #3    #4    #5    CTA
```

This is NOT a straight escalation. The "W" pattern creates two peaks (Slides 4 and 6) with a tonal cool-down at Slide 5 to prevent negativity overload. Research shows audiences hit a "negativity threshold" after 4-5 consecutive negative slides — Slide 5's empathetic tone resets emotional capacity.

## 3. Mistake Ordering — Emotional Arc Architecture

### Slide-by-Slide Emotional Blueprint

| Slide | Emotion | Mechanism | Micro-Cliffhanger |
|:-----:|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| 1 | Curiosity + mild threat | Open loop + loss aversion | "What are these mistakes?" |
| 2 | Recognition ("itu gue!") | Primacy + self-identification + yes-ladder | "...dan kamu bahkan gak sadar" |
| 3 | Discomfort | Commitment-consistency (already agreed with #1) | "Tapi yang ke-3 ini lebih parah..." |
| 4 | Surprise/shock | Dead-zone compensation + financial anchor | "...dan ini terjadi tiap hari" |
| 5 | Empathy + overwhelm | Negativity threshold management | "Ada satu lagi yang sering diabaikan..." |
| 6 | Urgency + desperation | Recency effect + all-loops-converge | "Capek? Ada cara yang lebih gampang." |
| 7 | Relief + empowerment | Peak-end rule + EPPM efficacy + dopamine rebound | Save trigger (checklist) |

### Key Principles

1. **Open Loops > Partial Resolution**: Never hint at the solution before Slide 7. Five unresolved problems create compounding Zeigarnik tension.

2. **Consequence-as-Cliffhanger**: Each mistake ends with its CONSEQUENCE (not just the mistake itself). The consequence makes the viewer think "apa lagi yang salah?" — which drives the next swipe.

3. **Pain relief = dopamine**: Research from PNAS shows pain relief activates the same reward circuitry as actual rewards. Five slides of threat → solution slide literally produces dopamine release. But ONLY if the preceding slides created genuine tension.

4. **Checklist = saves**: The checklist CTA format is 114% more likely to be saved than non-checklist formats. Each checkbox item mirrors one of the five mistakes, creating 1:1 resolution.

## 5. Hook Analysis & Alternatives

### Current Hook

"5 Kesalahan Fatal Saat Terima Order dari WA" — 9 words. Strong but testable.

### Hook Performance Prediction

| Hook Variant | Type | Predicted STR | Best For |
|-------------|------|:------------:|----------|
| "5 Kesalahan Fatal Saat Terima Order dari WA" | Number + Fear | HIGH | Saves (reference value) |

### Research Data

- "Mistakes" headlines get **63% higher CTR** than positive counterparts (Cognitive Clicks / negativity bias research)
- Loss aversion is **2x more powerful** than equivalent gain framing (Kahneman & Tversky)
- Odd numbers (3, 5, 7) outperform even numbers — perceived as more authentic
- The word "fatal" works when paired with solutions (EPPM meta-analysis of 248 fear appeal studies)

### Recommendation

Keep "5 Kesalahan Fatal Saat Terima Order dari WA" as the primary version. It combines three proven elements: number (5), fear word (fatal), and specificity (order dari WA).

Add sub-hook on Slide 1: "Yang ke-3 paling sering bikin rugi" — pre-loads curiosity for the dead-zone Slide 4.

For A/B testing, create a separate version with "Jangan Terima Order WA Sebelum Baca Ini" (prohibition hook — highest predicted STR due to psychological reactance).

## 6. Caption Optimization

### Caption Structure

TikTok truncates captions at ~80-100 characters. The first line must deliver the hook before the fold.

**Rules:**
- Total length: 150-300 characters (including hashtags)
- First 80 characters: hook + value proposition
- Do NOT repeat the slide hook verbatim — offer a complementary angle
- Do NOT put "link di bio" in the caption (save for Slide 7) — signals promotional intent, algorithm deprioritizes
- Use "kamu" (not "lo" or "Anda") — universal, warm, works across all Indonesian cities
- Max 2-3 emojis (authenticity signal)

### Recommended Caption

```
Yang jualan via WA, cek dulu sebelum rugi lagi 👇

Kesalahan kecil tapi bikin orderan berantakan dan profit bocor.

Yang mana yang paling sering kamu lakuin? Komen nomornya!

#tipsumkm #orderwa #jualanonline #umkm #catatorder
```

**Breakdown:**
- Line 1 (47 chars): Complementary hook — targets the person, not the content. "Cek dulu sebelum rugi lagi" creates urgency.
- Line 2: Value statement — frames the mistakes as "kecil tapi" (small but impactful).
- Line 3: Comment prompt — number response format (lowest friction).
- Hashtags: 5 total, niche-first ordering (see Section 8).
- Total: ~240 characters.

- Comment on 5-10 UMKM-related videos daily

**Days 4-7 (Light posting — 2-3 warm-up posts):**

Warm-up post ideas:
1. Simple text slide: "Siapa di sini yang jualan via WA? 🙋" (engagement bait, trains algorithm)
2. Relatable meme: "Buka WA jam 6 pagi, 47 chat belum dibales 😵" (humor, shareable)
3. Quick poll: "Order dari WA kamu catat di mana? A. Buku B. Excel C. Otak 🧠" (interaction driver)

Continue commenting on other accounts daily.

**Day 8+ (Launch the carousel):**
- Post "5 Kesalahan Fatal" as post #4 or #5
- Account has established signals: UMKM niche, business education, Indonesian audience
- Algorithm knows what type of audience to serve the content to

### Why Warm-Up Matters

- First videos from cold accounts are shown to only **200-500 viewers** in the initial test batch
- The algorithm needs at least 3-5 posts to understand the account's niche
- Engagement rate on warm accounts is 2-3x higher than cold accounts
- Your best content deserves a warmed-up audience, not a cold test batch

## 10. First-Hour Playbook

The first 60 minutes determine 70%+ of the post's distribution. TikTok shows the carousel to 200-500 viewers initially; if engagement is strong (15%+ interaction rate), it gets pushed to larger batches.

### Minute-by-Minute Plan

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| **T+0** (11:30 WIB) | Post the carousel. Add trending sound. |
| **T+1** | Share to 3-5 relevant WA groups (UMKM communities, seller groups). |
| **T+5** | Ask 5-10 contacts to comment with a number. Brief them: "Komen angka 1-5, yang mana relate." |
| **T+10** | Check first comments. Reply to EVERY one within minutes. Each reply doubles comment count. |
| **T+15** | Pin the most relatable/funny comment (e.g., "Semua 5 😭"). Pinned comments get more replies. |
| **T+20-60** | Stay active on TikTok. Engage with other content. Continue replying to all comments. |
| **T+60** | Check metrics: views, save rate, comment count. If >15% engagement rate → on track. |

### Do NOT

- Leave the app after posting (signals to algorithm that you're not engaged)
- Use "link di bio" in comments (looks spammy)
- Ask contacts to just "like" — comments are weighted 2-3x higher than likes
- Boost with paid promotion for this first carousel post (confuses the algorithm for new accounts)

## 11. A/B Testing Plan

### Rule: No Duplicate Content

TikTok uses deep learning + perceptual hashing to detect duplicates with 90%+ accuracy. Changing captions on the same images WILL trigger duplication flags. Penalties range from reach suppression to account restrictions.

### Testing Approach

Create **genuinely different versions** of the same topic:

| Week | Post | Format | Hook |
|------|------|--------|------|
| Week 1, Day 1 | Version 1 (carousel) | "5 Kesalahan Fatal Saat Terima Order dari WA" | Number + Fear |
| Week 1, Day 4 | Companion video (15s) | Talking-head covering same 5 mistakes | Personal authority |
| Week 2, Day 1 | Version 2 (carousel) | "Jangan Terima Order WA Sebelum Baca Ini" | Prohibition + Dare |
| Week 2, Day 4 | Solution carousel | "5 Cara Bener Catat Order WA Biar Gak Rugi" | Positive framing |

Each version uses **different slide designs** (different backgrounds, layouts, visual treatments) with overlapping but not identical content. Posted 3-5 days apart.

### Winning Variation Metrics (24-Hour)

| Metric | "Winning" Threshold | Why |
|--------|:------------------:|-----|
| Save rate | >5% of views | Strongest signal for educational content. #1 KPI. |
| Comment count | >20 in 24h | Audience interaction depth |
| Completion rate | >70% swipe-through | Content held attention |
| Share rate | >2% of views | Viral potential |
| View count | >1,000 in 24h | Baseline viability for new account |

**The single most important metric: Save rate.** If people are saving it, TikTok will keep pushing it for weeks/months. Save rate >5% = strong winner.

### Post-Test Action

- If a version gets 10K+ views: create 3 variations immediately (the algorithm is pushing)
- If a version gets <100 views after 48h: try different hook, keep same content structure
- Give each post 48-72 hours before evaluating — TikTok can resurface content days later

## 12. Canva Production Specs

### Template Setup

1. Create custom dimensions: **1080 x 1920 px**
2. Use "Presentation" mode — design all 7 slides as pages in one project
3. Set up brand kit:
   - Dark green: `#1B4332`
   - Gray-green: `#2D3E36`
   - Medium green: `#344E41`
   - Warning red: `#C0392B`
   - Warning orange: `#E67E22`
   - CTA green: `#16A34A`
   - Off-white: `#F8F9FA`
   - White: `#FFFFFF`
4. Create safe zone guides:
   - Add rectangles: 131px top, 367px bottom, 120px left, 120px right
   - Set to 10% opacity red
   - Lock these layers

### Fonts (Available in Canva Free)

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Hero numbers | League Gothic | Regular (already bold/condensed) |
| Slide titles | Montserrat | Bold (700) or ExtraBold (800) |
| Body text | DM Sans | Regular (400) |
| CTA/accent | Poppins | SemiBold (600) |

### Font Sizes (on 1080x1920 canvas)

| Element | Canvas Size | Minimum |
|---------|:----------:|:-------:|
| Hero number "5" | 180-220px | 160px |
| Slide titles | 72-90px | 60px |
| Body text | 42-48px | 36px |
| Small labels/captions | 30-36px | 28px |
| Stat callouts | 48-60px | 42px |

**Critical:** Text below 36px becomes hard to read after TikTok compression. Err larger.

### Typography Rules

- Line height (body): 1.4-1.6x font size
- Line height (headings): 1.2-1.3x font size
- Paragraph spacing: 30-40px between blocks
- Edge padding: 120px sides, 150px top (matches safe zone)
- Bold: 40-50% of visible text. Bold the mistake title + 1-2 key terms per slide.
- Never bold entire paragraphs.

### Export

- File type: **PNG** (sharper than JPG for text-heavy graphics)
- Quality: Default (Canva PNG = lossless)
- Do NOT use "Compress file" option
- Export all pages → upload 7 images to TikTok Photo Mode
- Verify: each image exactly 1080x1920px
- Color profile: sRGB (Canva default)

### Production Workflow

1. Design Slide 1 (hook) first — get it right before anything else
2. Design one content slide (Slide 2) as the master template
3. Duplicate Slide 2 four times for Slides 3-6
4. Modify content, accent colors, and icons on each duplicate
5. Design Slide 7 (CTA) separately with light background
6. Review all 7 in presentation mode for visual flow
7. Check readability at 50% zoom (simulates mobile compression)
8. Export as PNG

**Time estimate:** 45-60 minutes for first carousel (including screenshot prep). 30-45 minutes for subsequent carousels using the same template system.
