# TikTok Carousel Content Plan — CatatOrder

> Detailed post-by-post plan for TikTok image carousel content.
> Each post specifies: slide count, content per slide, slide order, format, and goal.

---

## Why Image Carousels (Not Just Video)

- TikTok actively promotes carousels on FYP to compete with Instagram (2025-2026 algorithm boost)
- 11% higher reach vs traditional video posts (Youthforia case study)
- Carousels generate higher **save rates** (algorithm gold — saves > likes in weight)
- $0 production: Canva templates + screenshots, no video editing needed
- Indonesian UMKM owners scroll TikTok during downtime — carousels are consumed faster than video
- Complementary to video content — doubles posting frequency without doubling effort

---

## Carousel Specs

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Dimensions** | 1080 x 1920 px (9:16 vertical) |
| **Max slides** | 35 |
| **Optimal slides** | 5-8 (aim for 100% swipe-through) |
| **File format** | PNG for graphics with text, JPG for photos |
| **Safe zone** | Top 131px + bottom 367px + sides 120px = avoid. Effective area: ~840 x 1422 px centered |
| **Text per slide** | 5-7 words for hook (slide 1), max 3 sentences for body slides |
| **Font** | Bold/medium weight (thin fonts blur after compression), left-aligned |
| **Music** | Add trending sound — even on carousels, music boosts discovery |

---

## Algorithm Signals (Ranked by Weight)

1. **Swipe-Through Rate (STR)** — % of viewers who swipe past slide 1. MOST IMPORTANT metric.
2. **Shares** — strongest endorsement signal, expands reach dramatically
3. **Saves** — "I want to come back" signal (educational content dominates here)
4. **Comments** — conversation signal, boosts visibility
5. **Likes** — baseline positive but lowest weight

**Implication:** Make slide 1 irresistible. Make final slide worth reaching. Create "saveable" content (tips, checklists, how-tos).

## Repurpose Strategy

Every TikTok carousel gets repurposed:

```
1 TikTok carousel → Instagram carousel (reformat to 1080x1080 or 1080x1350)
                  → Instagram Story (split into individual slides)
                  → Facebook Group post (first slide as image + text summary)
                  → WhatsApp Status (first 3 slides)
```

**1 production effort = 4-5 distribution touchpoints.**

---

## Production Workflow

1. **Write copy** for all slides in this document (already done above)
2. **Design in Canva:** Use 1080x1920 template, apply CatatOrder brand colors
3. **Screenshots:** Take real CatatOrder screenshots for tutorial posts (use demo data)
4. **Export:** PNG for each slide
5. **Upload to TikTok:** Add all slides + trending sound + caption + hashtags
6. **Cross-post:** Reformat for IG/FB/WA Status

**Time per carousel:** ~30-45 minutes (design + screenshot + upload + caption)
**Time per week (4 posts):** ~2-3 hours
