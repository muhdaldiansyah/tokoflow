# Lebaran 2026 Distribution Campaign

> 3 produk, 3 minggu, Rp0 budget.
> Ramadan: ~18 Feb | Lebaran: ~20 Mar 2026

---

## Strategic Logic

```
CatatOrder (gratis, wide net — tangkap semua UMKM)
    ↓ user grows, butuh fitur lebih
KueStrive (Rp29-69K, spesifik baker)
JahitStrive (Rp29-69K, spesifik tailor)
    ↓ setelah Lebaran, demand steady
ServisStrive + FotocopyStrive
```

**CatatOrder = net yang lebar. Kue + Jahit = upsell natural.**

### Why DM First, Not TikTok

- DM = reach kecil tapi conversion tinggi (10-20%)
- TikTok = reach besar tapi conversion unpredictable
- For first 10 users, DM is more reliable
- First users = testimoni = bahan konten TikTok yang real
- TikTok tanpa social proof = "aplikasi gak jelas". TikTok dengan testimoni = "orang udah pakai"

### Why Not SEO for Lebaran

- SEO butuh 4-12 minggu untuk ranking
- Window Lebaran = 3 minggu
- SEO for post-Lebaran (Servis HP, Fotocopy) — start now but not Lebaran focus

## Phase 1: Seeding — Direct Outreach (10-17 Feb)

> Target: 10 user pertama sebelum Ramadan mulai.

### Where to Find Targets

| Platform | Search Query | Target |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Instagram | "open order kue lebaran", "PO nastar" | 30 bakers |
| Instagram | "buka slot jahit lebaran", "jahit baju lebaran" | 20 tailors |
| TikTok | "orderan kue numpuk", "home baker" | 15 bakers |
| TikTok | "jahit lebaran", "penjahit rumahan" | 10 tailors |
| Facebook | Grup "Home Baker Indonesia", "Penjahit Indonesia" | Join 5 groups |

### Signs They're Overwhelmed (= Perfect Timing)

- Story penuh screenshot WA orderan
- Post "slot tinggal X"
- Keluhan "capek ngetik ulang pesanan"
- Harga list di-copy paste berulang

### DM Scripts

**For Baker:**
> Kak, aku lihat orderan kue Lebarannya rame banget! Aku bikin tools gratis buat manage orderan dari WA — pesanan masuk, status produksi, sampai kirim struk otomatis ke customer. Gak perlu download app, langsung dari browser. Mau coba? Gratis, 60 detik langsung bisa pakai. [link]

**For Tailor:**
> Kak, lagi banyak orderan jahit Lebaran ya? Aku bikin tools gratis buat tracking pesanan jahit — dari terima ukuran, potong, jahit, sampai finishing. Customer bisa lihat progress via link, gak perlu bales WA satu-satu. Mau coba? [link]

**Rules:**
- Send pagi (7-9) or malam (19-21) — when they check their phone
- 1 DM per person. If no reply in 3 days, move on.
- Personalize — mention their product name if possible
- Target: 10 DM/hari, 50 total for the week

## Phase 2: Ramadan Week 1-2 — Content Push (18 Feb - 4 Mar)

> Pain is REAL now. Content that mirrors their experience today resonates.

### 5 TikTok/Reels Videos

**Video 1: "POV: Orderan kue Lebaran numpuk"** (Day 1 Ramadan)
- Screen recording scroll WA chat penuh order → cut to dashboard rapi
- Hook: Relatable chaos | 15-30 detik

**Video 2: "1 order hilang = Rp500K melayang"** (Day 3)
- Loss aversion story → show dashboard | 30-45 detik

**Video 3: "Struk digital dari HP, gak perlu printer"** (Day 5)
- Tutorial demo: buat pesanan → generate struk → kirim WA | 30 detik

**Video 4: "Penjahit ini tracking 200 orderan Lebaran dari HP"** (Day 7)
- Case study (demo data) → dashboard | 30-45 detik

**Video 5: "Beda kelola order di WA vs pakai app"** (Day 10)
- Side by side: WA chaos vs dashboard | 15-30 detik

### User Onboarding (Ongoing)

Every new user who signs up:
1. WA personal: "Hai kak, terima kasih sudah coba! Ada yang bisa dibantu setup?"
2. Help them input first order — once 1 order is in, they're hooked
3. Ask for dashboard screenshot → use as testimonial content
4. Ask them to share to friends: "Kalau ada teman yang butuh, boleh share ya"

## Phase 3: Ramadan Week 3-4 — Conversion (5-20 Mar)

### Referral Push
- Active users → "Kak, ada teman yang juga terima orderan Lebaran? Share link ini"
- Each active user can refer 2-3 people (bakers/tailors know each other)
- Target: 2x users from referral (10 → 20 → 40)

### Viral Loop Activation
- Check: Is CatatOrder branding footer visible enough on receipts?
- Check: Is the link clickable in WA messages?
- If not — fix immediately

### Conversion to Paid (Soft)
- Free tier stays during Ramadan — DON'T paywall during peak rush
- After Lebaran, users who depend on tool → natural conversion
- "Slot gratis tinggal X" — real scarcity, not fake
