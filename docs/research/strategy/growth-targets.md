## DGE 3-Layer System

Growth follows 3 sequential layers:

```
LAYER 1: SEEDING (Month 1-2)
  Manual, high-touch, direct outreach.
  Target: 50-100 first users + feedback + testimonials.

LAYER 2: CONTENT ENGINE (Month 2-6)
  Organic content (SEO + TikTok) that works 24/7.
  Target: 100-500 users/month from content.

LAYER 3: RETENTION LOOP (Ongoing)
  Systems that keep users active and paying.
  Target: Churn < 5%/month. LTV grows.
```

**Rule: Layers must be built sequentially.** Can't skip to SEO (Layer 2) without 20 active users giving feedback (Layer 1).

## Stage-by-Stage Playbook

### Stage 1: 0 → 100 Users (Month 1-2)

**Focus:** Layer 1 (Seeding) + Onboarding fix

**Daily activities (1-2 hours):**
- 15 min: Answer questions in 3 FB groups
- 15 min: Follow up new users via WA
- 30 min: Create 1 TikTok/Reel
- 30 min: Fix product based on feedback

**Weekly activities:**
- Post in 3-5 FB groups (value + mention tool)
- Forward to 5 WA groups
- Collect 1-2 testimonials
- Review metrics: signup, active, feedback

**Exit milestone:** 100 signups, 30+ active, 3+ paying
- If NOT reached after 2 months: evaluate demand, onboarding, pricing

### Stage 2: 100 → 500 Users (Month 2-6)

**Focus:** Layer 2 (Content Engine) + Layer 1 continues

**Weekly activities:**
- 1 SEO article (1,000-2,000 words, target 1 keyword)
- 3-5 TikTok/Reels
- 15 min/day community presence
- Follow up new users via WA
- Weekly metrics review

**Monthly activities:**
- 4 SEO articles published
- Review: which channel produces most signups?
- Double down on best channel
- Send monthly value report to all active users
- Ask 5 happy users for referral

**Exit milestone:** 500 signups, 100+ active, 20+ paying

### Stage 3: 500 → 1,000 Users (Month 6-12)

**Focus:** Layer 2 (scale) + Layer 3 (retention) + Optimize conversion

**Activities:**
- 2 SEO articles/week
- Programmatic SEO pages (50+ pages)
- TikTok/Reels every day
- Automated WA onboarding sequence (Fonnte)
- Monthly value report automated
- Referral system live
- A/B test pricing and paywall placement
- Partner with 2-3 UMKM communities

**Exit milestone:** 1,000 signups, 200+ active, 50+ paying

## Retention Metrics Targets

| Metric | Target | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Day-1 retention (login next day) | > 40% | < 20% |
| Day-7 retention | > 25% | < 10% |
| Day-30 retention | > 15% | < 5% |
| Monthly churn (paid users) | < 5% | > 10% |
| Time to aha moment | < 5 min | > 15 min |

### Why Retention > Acquisition

```
Scenario A: 100 new users/month, 10% churn
  Month 12: ~380 users (plateau)

Scenario B: 50 new users/month, 3% churn
  Month 12: ~480 users (still growing, overtakes A)
```
