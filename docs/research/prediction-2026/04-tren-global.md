## 6. Emerging Market SaaS Patterns

### Comparable Markets

| Negara | SaaS Growth | Mobile Traffic | Key Insight |
|--------|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| **India** | 24% CAGR, >$15B FY24 | ~75% | Khatabook model: free tool → financial services |
| **Brazil** | Massive WA adoption | ~70% | 96% bisnis pakai WA sebagai tool utama |
| **Nigeria** | SaaS ~15% | 86.67% | Mobile-money integration kritis |
| **Philippines** | Sampai 30% | ~85% | Digital services cross-border |
| **Vietnam** | Strong SaaS | 86.51% | Vertical SaaS di logistics/manufacturing |

### Lessons dari Khatabook (India) & BukuKas/BukuWarung

**Yang BERHASIL:**
- Free-to-premium model: free basic → monetize via financial services
- Offline-first design
- Self-guided onboarding (simple UI, tanpa training)
- BukuWarung: tidak charge subscription, hanya charge setelah merchant lewati volume tertentu

**Yang GAGAL:**
- **BukuKas ($140M): bangkrut Sep 2023** despite Sequoia backing
- **Khatabook ($187M funding): revenue hanya Rs 1.39 Cr (~$167K)** per Maret 2025 — massive funding-to-revenue mismatch
- **Khatabook cabut dari Indonesia** (BukuUang ditutup)
- Monetisasi tetap **core challenge** — "free trojan horse" kumpulkan data tapi convert ke revenue sangat sulit

**Lesson untuk CatatOrder:** Free-to-acquire works untuk growth tapi jebakan monetisasi. Revenue model jelas dari awal (Rp15K pack) = lebih sustainable dari "$100M+ free everything" approach.

### Pricing Strategies untuk Low-Income Markets

| Strategi | Data |
|----------|------|
| Usage-based/hybrid | 46% SaaS firms kombinasi subscription + variable |
| 80% buyers | bilang usage-based "better aligns with value" |
| Credit-based model | 126% YoY growth (79 dari top 500 SaaS) |
| Pay-as-you-go | Customer mulai minimal, bayar sesuai usage |
| Missing local payment | Bisa kehilangan **60% customers** di checkout |

**CatatOrder's sachet model (Rp15K per 50 pesanan) = perfectly aligned** dengan emerging market pricing best practices.
