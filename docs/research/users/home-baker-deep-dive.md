# Deep Dive: Home Baker Kue Custom in Indonesia

> The #1 priority target for CatatOrder. Comprehensive research on ibu rumah tangga who sell kue kering, kue custom, and homemade baked goods via WhatsApp.

*Research date: 2026-02-13*

---

## 1. Market Size & Business Economics

### How Many Home Bakers?

No single BPS figure isolates "home bakers," but the data converges on a massive number:

- Indonesia has **65.6 million UMKM** units total (Dec 2024), 99.9% of all businesses. ([UKMINDONESIA.ID](https://ukmindonesia.id/baca-deskripsi-posts/data-umkm-jumlah-dan-pertumbuhan-usaha-mikro-kecil-dan-menengah-di-indonesia))
- The accommodation and food service sector alone has **6.40 million business units**. ([GoodStats](https://goodstats.id/article/lebih-dari-10000-usaha-kuliner-ada-di-indonesia-bagaimana-statistiknya-OTIU5))
- **64.5% of UMKM operators are women** (BPS 2024) — approximately 37 million women. Top 3 sub-sectors for women: fashion, **culinary**, and kriya (crafts). ([BacaIni](https://bacaini.id/perempuan-kuasai-umkm-female-breadwinner-indonesia/))
- BPS Manufacturing Industry report (2021): **829 registered** bakery/cake companies (medium + large). Unregistered home-based bakers are orders of magnitude larger. ([6WResearch](https://www.6wresearch.com/industry-report/indonesia-bakery-market))
- In **Kampung Kue** (Surabaya) alone, one neighborhood has **63 cake entrepreneurs**, almost all women/housewives. ([LinkUMKM](https://linkumkm.id/news/detail/12061/sukses-bangun-bisnis-kue-begini-cerita-choirul-mahpuduah))
- Home bakery segment **grew significantly during COVID-19**. Google Trends: searches for "home business ideas" increased **300%+** and "food business ideas" increased **250%** in 12 months. ([Kompas](https://www.kompas.com/food/read/2021/02/22/220200575/tren-usaha-makanan-rumahan-meningkat-drastis-saat-pandemi))
- APEBI (Indonesian Bakery Producer Association): bakery industry grows **above 10% annually**. ([6WResearch](https://www.6wresearch.com/industry-report/indonesia-bakery-market))

**Estimated home baker population:** Conservatively **500K-1M+** women running home-based baking businesses across Indonesia, with millions more doing it seasonally during Lebaran.

### Cost Structure

**Modal awal (startup):** Rp500K - Rp5 juta depending on whether they already own an oven/mixer. ([UKMINDONESIA.ID](https://ukmindonesia.id/baca-deskripsi-posts/modal-500-ribu-bisa-bisnis-kue-kering-begini-cara-memulainya))

**Per-toples production cost:**
- Bahan baku (flour, butter, eggs, sugar, cheese, jam): ~Rp30,000-40,000/toples
- Gas/electricity: ~Rp4,000-5,000/toples
- Packaging (toples + label + ribbon): ~Rp10,000-15,000/toples
- **Total production cost: ~Rp51,000/toples** ([Bisnis LovelyBogor](https://bisnis.lovelybogor.com/cara-menentukan-harga-jual-kue-kering-rumahan/))

**Bulk raw material costs:**
- Bahan baku per batch: Rp1.5-2.5 juta
- Kemasan: Rp200K-500K per batch
- Total monthly operational: Rp9.5 juta for ~Rp15 juta revenue business ([Paper.id](https://www.paper.id/blog/ide-bisnis/usaha-modal-kecil-untung-besar/))

### Profit Margins

- **Standard margin:** 30-50% markup on production cost is typical ([BFI Finance](https://www.bfi.co.id/id/blog/tips-bisnis-kue-kering-dan-estimasi-keuntungannya))
- **Premium segment:** Kue kering sold at Rp100K+ per toples can yield **100% margin** because ingredient cost difference is minimal ([Kompas Food](https://www.kompas.com/food/read/2021/04/21/160400375/berapa-keuntungan-bisnis-kue-kering-selama-ramadhan-?page=all))
- **Mass market:** Kue at Rp40-65K per toples yields ~30% margin
- For 100 toples/month at Rp10-20K profit per toples: Rp1-2 juta net ([Liputan6](https://www.liputan6.com/hot/read/6234902/20-ide-jualan-kue-kering-modal-kecil-untuk-ibu-rumah-tangga-untung-melimpah-dari-dapur-sendiri))

## 2. Daily Workflow & Pain Points

### Step-by-Step Order Flow for a Typical Ibu Kue

1. **Receive order via WhatsApp** — customer sends: "Mbak mau pesan nastar 5 toples, kastengel 3 toples, kirim ke [alamat], Lebaran ya"
2. **Confirm availability and price** — reply with price per toples and total
3. **Request DP (down payment)** — typically 50% via bank transfer (BCA/Mandiri/BRI)
4. **Record order** — usually in a **buku tulis (notebook)**, sometimes a screenshot of the WA chat, occasionally Excel or Google Sheets
5. **Purchase bahan baku** — go to the market or order online based on accumulated orders
6. **Production** — baking in batches, often starting at dawn and finishing late at night during peak season
7. **Packaging** — into toples, labeled, sometimes with ribbon/sticker
8. **Delivery coordination** — pick-up, self-delivery, or courier (Grab/GoSend/Paxel)
9. **Collect remaining payment** — the other 50% on delivery/pickup
10. **Send receipt/confirmation** — usually just a WA message saying "sudah dikirim ya Mbak"

### What Goes Wrong — Real Pain Points

**Manual recording causes errors:**
- "Pawon Kue menggunakan buku untuk mencatat pesanan dan penjualan produk. Metode tersebut telah digunakan sejak perusahaan didirikan, namun perusahaan mengalami kesulitan saat menggunakan metode ini." ([OJS UNP Kediri](https://ojs.unpkediri.ac.id/index.php/intensif/article/download/12122/965))
- "Catatan manual bisa hilang, ingatan aplikasi pasti cemerlang." ([Nutapos](https://nutapos.com/aplikasi-kasir-toko-kue/))
- "Pencatatan penjualan masih menggunakan sistem manual yang masih dicatat menggunakan buku laporan yang mengakibatkan kurang efektif dalam melihat laporan penjualan." ([Jurnal BSI](https://jurnal.bsi.ac.id/index.php/justian/article/view/9311))

**Peak season chaos:**
- **Arum Cookies (Surabaya):** "Pemilik mengaku kewalahan dan telah menolak banyak orderan." Had to convert living room into production space, increase staff from 2 to 6, extend hours until 10 PM. Minimum daily target: 100 jars. ([Petisi.co](https://petisi.co/kue-kering-lebaran-banjir-pesanan-pemilik-arum-cookies-surabaya-kualahan-layani-permintaan/))
- **Mama Aira (Samarinda):** "pekerjaan produksi berlangsung lama dari pagi buta sampai malam", must process **1,400 toples**. ([Diskominfo Kaltim](https://diskominfo.kaltimprov.go.id/index.php/ekonomi/kisah-pengusaha-kue-kering-rumahan-mama-aira-banjir-orderan))
- **Farah Tri Maharani (Jakarta):** By mid-Ramadan already had **3,000 toples** orders — "membuat kewalahan." ([ANTARA](https://www.antaranews.com/berita/3484035/usaha-kue-kering-di-jakarta-timur-kebanjiran-pesanan-menjelang-lebaran))
- **Banyumas seller:** Received 500 toples orders each time she opened orders, "mulai kewalahan." ([TribunBanyumas](https://banyumas.tribunnews.com/2022/04/27/permintaan-jelang-lebaran-meningkat-penjual-kue-kering-di-plana-banyumas-mulai-kewalahan))
- **Rosidah (DKU, Jombang):** Had to **reject 30% of orders** worth Rp300-400 million because she could not fulfill them. ([Detik Finance](https://finance.detik.com/solusiukm/d-2326961/kisah-pengusaha-kue-kering-rumahan-raup-omzet-rp-1-miliar-saat-lebaran))

### Payment Handling

- **DP (Down Payment)** is standard: typically 50% upfront via bank transfer
- **Transfer bank** is the dominant payment method (BCA, Mandiri, BRI most common)
- **COD** for local pickup
- **E-wallet** (GoPay, OVO, DANA) increasingly used but not dominant
- **QRIS** adopted by some market sellers ([Liputan6](https://www.liputan6.com/hot/read/6275874/tips-jualan-kue-kering-tanpa-modal-agar-tetap-untung-dan-laris-manis))
- Tracking paid vs not paid is done **manually in the buku tulis** or in memory — this is a major pain point

## 3. WhatsApp Behavior

### How Customers Order Kue via WA

Typical customer messages:

> "Mbak, mau pesan kue kering buat Lebaran ya. Nastar 5 toples, kastengel 3, putri salju 2. Kirim ke [alamat]. Kapan bisa ready?"

> "Hai, saya mau pesan hampers Lebaran isi 6 toples. Ada paket berapa?"

> "Bu mau order nastar 10 toples ya, mau kirim ke Surabaya. DP berapa?"

Format is almost always **unstructured free text** — no consistent format, mixed with questions, emoji, voice notes. Some sellers try to enforce a format but customers rarely follow it. ([Jawaraspeed](https://www.jawaraspeed.com/2023/10/20-contoh-format-order-makanan-apapun-kusus-di-wa.html), [DOKU Blog](https://www.doku.com/en-us/blog/cara-membuat-format-order-whatsapp))

### Chat Volume During Lebaran

Based on the case studies:
- A baker with 1,400-3,000 toples represents **200-500 individual customers** sending multiple messages each
- Each order exchange involves 5-15 messages (inquiry, confirmation, DP confirmation, production update, delivery coordination)
- During peak, a busy home baker may handle **50-200+ active WA conversations** simultaneously

### WA Business vs Regular WA

Most home bakers use **WhatsApp Business** for its catalog feature, quick replies, and business profile. One case study showed orders increasing **50% in 2 months** after setting up a WA Business catalog. ([DOKU Blog](https://www.doku.com/en-us/blog/cara-membuat-format-order-whatsapp), [Qontak](https://qontak.com/blog/cara-membuat-katalog-di-wa/))

### Current Organization Methods

1. **Buku tulis (handwritten notebook)** — most common
2. **Screenshots of WA chats** — saved to phone gallery
3. **Notes app on phone**
4. **Google Sheets / Excel** — for the more tech-savvy
5. **Google Forms** — some use forms shared via WA link
6. **Memory** — especially for smaller bakers with < 20 orders

### WA Groups and Broadcast Lists

- Sellers use **WA Broadcast lists** to announce new products, open pre-orders, share Lebaran menus
- Some sell through **WA Groups** (neighborhood groups, arisan groups, school parent groups)
- Community WA groups like Kampung Kue coordinate production among neighboring bakers

### Why Most Do NOT Use Digital Tools

1. **Low digital literacy:** Only **18% of UMKM operators** have basic digital skills (Kominfo 2024). ([Kompasiana](https://www.kompasiana.com/rifkyaryo3883/66dda373c925c45a834adf73/kurangnya-pengetahuan-digital-pada-umkm-menjadi-tantangan-dalam-era-digital))
2. **Cost concerns:** Rp200-300K/month POS is too expensive for Rp5-15 juta/month revenue
3. **Fear of scams/security:** "Ketakutan akan penipuan" is a barrier. ([Cashlez](https://cashlez.com/blog/banyak-pelaku-umkm-yang-belum-go-digital-ternyata-ini-alasannya_637.html))
4. **Time/energy constraints:** "Terbatasnya waktu maupun energi untuk memikirkan inovasi usaha dan mencoba teknologi baru" ([Cashlez](https://cashlez.com/blog/banyak-pelaku-umkm-yang-belum-go-digital-ternyata-ini-alasannya_637.html))
5. **"Buku tulis works fine"** mentality — until Lebaran chaos hits
6. **~35% of Indonesian UMKM** have not yet leveraged digital technology optimally. ([DJKN Kemenkeu](https://www.djkn.kemenkeu.go.id/kpknl-kendari/baca-artikel/17371/Meningkatkan-UMKM-dengan-Teknologi-Informasi-Langkah-Menuju-Era-Digital.html))
7. **40% of UMKM** lack adequate access to digital training and knowledge. ([SmartLegal](https://smartlegal.id/galeri-hukum/lainnya/2024/12/11/10-permasalahan-yang-sering-terjadi-pada-umkm-sl/))

### Instagram vs WA vs TikTok

Instagram is for **showcase and discovery**; WhatsApp is where **actual ordering happens**. Flow: customer sees product on Instagram -> DMs or clicks WA link in bio -> orders via WA. TikTok is increasingly part of the discovery funnel.

TikTok content types among home bakers:
- Recipe tutorials
- "Behind the scenes" production videos
- Packing/hampers aesthetic videos
- "Banjir orderan" flex videos

## 6. Lebaran Seasonal Pattern (CRITICAL)

### Timeline for Ramadan/Lebaran 2026

| Date | Event |
|------|-------|
| **Feb 17, 2026** | Sidang Isbat (government determines start) |
| **Feb 18 or 19, 2026** | 1 Ramadan 1447 H (start of fasting) |
| **March 20 or 21, 2026** | Idul Fitri 1447 H (Lebaran) |

Sources: [Detik Hikmah](https://www.detik.com/hikmah/khazanah/d-8351202/kapan-hari-pertama-puasa-2026-dimulai-simak-jadwal-lengkapnya), [KapanLagi](https://plus.kapanlagi.com/ramadan-2026-tanggal-berapa-ini-jadwal-puasa-38-libur-sekolah-3d09f3.html)

### Pre-Order Timeline

- **H-30 to H-45 (early-mid January):** Smart home bakers start accepting pre-orders
- **Start of Ramadan (Feb 18-19):** Major wave of orders begins

## 7. Psychology & Decision Making

### What Motivates an Ibu Kue to Try a New Tool?

1. **Pain first:** She will try when her current system breaks — lost order, mixed-up payment, customer complains. Lebaran is when pain is maximum.
2. **Free is essential:** She will not pay upfront for something unproven. "Masa awal" free approach is correct.
3. **WhatsApp integration is non-negotiable:** If it does not work with WA, she will not use it.
4. **Simplicity:** "Kurangnya kemampuan pengguna dalam mengoperasikan aplikasi digital dasar." The tool must be simpler than Excel.
5. **Peer recommendation:** 93% of consumers trust friends/family recommendations. ([SAPX Express](https://www.sapx.id/blog/word-of-mouth-marketing/))

### What Makes Her Recommend to Friends?

- **Solves a real pain** she experienced (lost order, payment confusion)
- **Saves visible time** — "look, all my orders are organized"
- **Makes her look professional** — digital receipts, neat status tracking
- **Free or very cheap** — she would never recommend something expensive to fellow ibu-ibu
- **Word of mouth is the #1 distribution channel.** "Nama yang simpel akan memudahkan promosi dari mulut ke mulut." ([Mekari Jurnal](https://www.jurnal.id/id/blog/2018-5-strategi-sukses-memasarkan-bisnis-kue/))

### Trust Factors

- **Free to use** (no credit card)
- **Google login** (familiar)
- **Indonesian language** (absolutely critical)
- **Simple and clean UI** (no overwhelming features)
- **WhatsApp-native** (she lives in WA)
- **Peer endorsement** (if her ibu-ibu network uses it)

### Price Sensitivity

- **Rp49K/month** is achievable for bakers earning Rp5-15 juta/month (0.3-1% of revenue) — BUT only after proven value
- BukuKas/BukuWarung are free. POS at Rp250-300K/month is "too expensive."
- Free tier with 150 orders covers most bakers in normal months. Forces upgrade only during Lebaran peak when they're making money.

### Social Proof Importance

Extremely important. Indonesian UMKM women make business decisions within **arisan groups, WA groups, and neighborhood networks**. One satisfied user in a group of 30-60 women can drive adoption across the entire network.
