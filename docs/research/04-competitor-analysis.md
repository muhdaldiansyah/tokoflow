# Competitor Analysis: Order-to-Payment Flows

> 17+ platforms analyzed — Indonesian and international

---

## Indonesian Platforms

### QuickOrder.ai
- **Model:** Smart keyboard overlay for WhatsApp
- **Order flow:** Sellers reply to chats, record orders, send invoices from keyboard — no app switching
- **Payment:** Invoice generation, manual confirmation. No integrated gateway
- **Pricing:** Free app (Play Store)
- **Key pattern:** Keyboard-as-interface — zero context switch from WhatsApp

### Dazo.id
- **Model:** AI chatbot + order management + digital store
- **Order flow:** WhatsApp AI Bot auto-receives/processes/responds to orders across WA, Instagram, Facebook, Telegram
- **Payment:** Integrated with payment + delivery services. Auto-notifications
- **Pricing:** 14-day free trial, tiered plans
- **Key pattern:** AI handles order intake across multiple channels

### Opaper.app
- **Model:** F&B-focused QR ordering + POS
- **Order flow:** QR scan at table → browse menu → order → pay
- **Payment:** **Xendit integration** — QRIS, e-wallets, cards. Split bill. Auto-printed receipts
- **Pricing:** **Rp500,000 one-time** (no monthly fees) — very attractive to cost-sensitive UMKM
- **Key pattern:** One-time payment model + Xendit for payment acceptance

### Pawoon
- **Model:** Cloud POS on Android
- **Payment:** Cash, cards, GoPay, OVO, DANA
- **Pricing:** Free tier; Basic Rp299K/month; Pro Rp599K/month
- **Key pattern:** Loyalty programs built into POS

### Majoo
- **Model:** End-to-end SaaS (POS + online ordering + consumer app)
- **Payment:** All e-wallets, QRIS, paylater. Smart EDC device
- **Pricing:** Starter Rp149K/month → Prime Rp599K/month
- **Key pattern:** Smart EDC hardware = order + payment in one device

### iSeller
- **Model:** Omnichannel — POS, eCommerce, marketplaces, social media, GrabFood
- **Payment:** iSeller Pay (all cards + e-wallets). Consolidated settlements
- **Pricing:** Starter Rp250K/month (500 products, 1000 orders)
- **Key pattern:** "Start accepting online orders in 5 minutes" — fast setup

### Jubelio
- **Model:** Omnichannel (back office + warehouse + marketplace + webstore + POS)
- **Payment:** Cash, debit/credit, e-wallet, QRIS via POS
- **Pricing:** **Rp150/order** (no limits on features/users/locations)
- **Key pattern:** Per-order pricing — aligned incentives with seller growth

### TokoTalk
- **Model:** Social commerce + WhatsApp Assistant
- **Payment:** 3 bank accounts as payment options (premium). Digital payments
- **Pricing:** Free + Pro tier
- **Key pattern:** Chrome extension on WhatsApp Web auto-converts chats to orders

### BukuWarung / CrediBook
- **Model:** Started as bookkeeping apps → pivoted
- **BukuWarung:** Agent payment platform (pulsa, electricity tokens)
- **CrediBook/CrediMart:** COD + BNPL for wholesale
- **Key pattern:** Merchants earn commission selling digital products

### SIRCLO
- **Model:** Omnichannel commerce enabler for enterprise brands
- **Payment:** Marketplace + WhatsApp Business API
- **Key pattern:** Enterprise-grade serving 1M+ UMKMs

### Orderfaz
- **Model:** Smart WhatsApp Keyboard (like QuickOrder) + browser plugin
- **Payment:** Invoice/checkout links. Transaction tracking with fraud monitoring
- **Key pattern:** Claims 30-200% sales conversion improvement

## International Platforms

### Dukaan (India) ⭐
- **Model:** Mobile-first store builder
- **Order flow:** Browse catalog → 3-step checkout → receive order on WhatsApp
- **Payment:** **Zero transaction fees**. UPI, bank, debit (0.99%). COD option
- **Pricing:** Starting Rs6,999/year (~Rp1.3M)
- **Key pattern:** "Launch online store in 30 seconds" + zero-fee payments

### Interakt (India) ⭐⭐
- **Model:** WhatsApp Business API platform
- **Order flow:** Send order value → collect shipping → share payment links — **all inside WhatsApp chat**
- **Payment:** **Razorpay integration** — dynamic payment links, UPI, cards, wallets. Real-time status tracking. Auto-confirmation in WhatsApp
- **Pricing:** Growth $49/month
- **Key pattern:** Complete purchase loop inside WhatsApp. Dynamic payment links with real-time status

### Take.app (Global)
- **Model:** Online store for WhatsApp, 180+ countries
- **Payment:** 100+ local methods (Stripe, PayPal, Xendit). Lower fees, instant payouts
- **Key pattern:** **Automatic follow-up on unpaid orders** to boost revenue

### Square Online (US)
- **Order flow:** Browse → cart → checkout with smart sequencing
- **Confirmation:** Order type, status, shipping/pickup details, items, store policies
- **Payment:** Integrated Square. Cards, tap, mobile. Next-day deposits
- **Pricing:** Free tier + 2.9% + $0.30
- **Key pattern:** Unified Order Manager across online + POS channels

### Toast (US Restaurants) ⭐
- **Order flow:** Cloud POS. Online ordering auto-fires to kitchen. Menu syncs real-time
- **Payment:** Cards, tap, mobile. **Zero commission on online orders** (vs 15-30% delivery apps)
- **Pricing:** Starter $0/month + 2.49-3.09% processing
- **Key pattern:** "Own your customer relationship" — no marketplace commission

### Chatfuel / Tidio
- **Model:** No-code chatbots for Messenger/Instagram/WhatsApp
- **Key pattern:** Abandoned cart recovery flows, product recommendations in chat

## Key Trends (2024-2026)

### Social Commerce Payments
- In-app payments growing **30% CAGR** ($19.1B → $94.5B by 2030)
- In-chat payments: **35% higher conversion**, **5x ROAS**
- Complete purchase loop in one chat thread

### WhatsApp Commerce
- 3.03 billion users, **98% open rate**
- Gen Z prefers WhatsApp over email/Instagram for brands
- **WhatsApp Flows:** 158% higher conversion than web forms, 80%+ completion rates
- WhatsApp Pay: active in India (UPI) and Brazil — NOT in Indonesia yet

### QRIS Dominance in Indonesia
- **57+ million users**, 38+ million merchants (93% UMKM)
- **175% YoY transaction volume surge** in 2024
- **0% MDR** for micro transactions < Rp500K
- Cross-border: Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore

### Auto Bank Mutation Checking
- Services like **Moota.co**, **Mesinotomatis.com**, **Mutasibank.co.id**
- Auto-check bank statements → webhook when payment arrives
- Moota: 7,200+ businesses, ~Rp1,500/day per account
- **Middle ground** — no full gateway, but automated verification
