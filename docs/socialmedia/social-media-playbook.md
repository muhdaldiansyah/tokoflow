## Algorithm Cheat Sheet

### What the Algorithm Rewards (Both Platforms)

| Signal | Weight | How to Trigger |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| **Saves** | 10x likes | Checklists, templates, tips lists — "save-worthy" content |
| **Shares/DM Sends** | 7x likes (TT), 3-5x likes (IG) | Relatable pain points, useful tips people forward |
| **Comments** | 5x likes | Ask questions, "komen nomornya!", debate prompts |
| **Watch time / Swipe-through** | Highest overall | Strong hook, curiosity gap, numbered progression |
| **Likes** | 1x (baseline) | Lowest value — don't optimize for likes |

### TikTok-Specific (2026)

- **Follower-first testing:** Your followers are the test panel. First followers matter enormously.
- **200-view jail:** New accounts get 200-300 view test batches. Need 12-15 consistent videos over 3-6 weeks to escape.
- **Qualified views:** Views must last 5+ seconds to count (new 2026 metric).
- **Carousels:** 81% higher engagement, 3x more saves than video. Algorithm tracks swipe-through + dwell time per slide.
- **On-screen text:** Videos with text rank 400% higher in TikTok search (OCR indexing).
- **Critical 15-min window:** Engagement in first 15 minutes determines broader distribution.

### Instagram-Specific (2026)

- **"Second Chance" re-serve:** If someone scrolls past your carousel, IG shows it again starting from **slide 2**. Make slide 2 a second hook.
- **DM shares:** Most powerful signal for reaching NEW audiences (Adam Mosseri confirmed).
- **Originality:** Original content gets 40-60% more distribution. 10+ reposts in 30 days = excluded from recommendations.
- **Reels for discovery:** 30.81% average reach rate. Best for small accounts under 50K.
- **Carousels for engagement:** 1.92% engagement (4x Reels). Highest saves of any format.
- **Comment quality:** Multi-sentence comments and back-and-forth threads weighted far more than emoji/single-word.

### Distribution Phases (How Your Post Spreads)

```
Phase 1: 200-500 viewers (first 0-3 hours)
   ↓ needs 40%+ completion + 50 engagement points
Phase 2: ~1,000 viewers
   ↓ needs 50%+ completion + 10%+ engagement rate
Phase 3: 2,000-10,000 viewers (wider FYP)
   ↓ sustained strong metrics
Phase 4: 10,000+ (viral push)
   ↓ engagement must sustain at each wave
Phase 5: Plateau / natural decline
```

## What KILLS Your Reach (Don't Do These)

### Account-Killing Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Kills You |
|---------|-----------------|
| Post immediately after account creation | Algorithm has no context, pushes to wrong audience |
| Use automation tools/bots | Instagram detects within hours. 67% lower reach even 1 YEAR later |
| Follow/unfollow rapidly (F4F) | Immediate spam flag, possible temporary ban |
| Post TikTok videos with watermark on Instagram | Instagram actively deprioritizes watermarked content |
| Buy followers/likes/comments | Fake engagement easily detected, can result in permanent ban |

### Growth-Killing Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Kills You |
|---------|-----------------|
| Inconsistent posting | Algorithm stops recommending your content |
| No niche focus (posting random topics) | Algorithm can't categorize you, shows to wrong audience |
| Ignoring comments on your posts | Lose 21-42% potential engagement boost |
| "Link di bio" in caption/comments | Algorithm deprioritizes, looks spammy |
| "Like for part 2" engagement bait | TikTok specifically flags and penalizes this |
| Using 30 hashtags (IG) | Flagged as spammy in 2026 |
| Same hashtag set on every post | Looks automated, triggers spam detection |
| Leaving TikTok right after posting | Signals disengagement to algorithm |
| Deleting poor-performing posts | Flags suspicious behavior, disrupts algorithm learning |
| Post more than 3x/day in first 2 weeks | New accounts posting aggressively get flagged |

### Shadowban Signs & Recovery

**Signs:** Sudden 80%+ view drop, posts not appearing under hashtags, zero Explore/FYP distribution.

**Recovery:**
1. Stop ALL activity for 48-72 hours
2. Remove third-party apps
3. Resume at 50% normal activity
4. Post high-quality, original content only
5. Recovery: 3-14 days (typical), up to 90 days (severe)

## WA Group Seeding Strategy

### Why WhatsApp Matters
- 90.8% of Indonesian internet users use WhatsApp
- UMKM owners live on WA — it's their primary business tool
- WA groups are high-trust environments with direct access to target users

### How to Seed

1. **Join 10-20 WA groups** (UMKM, bisnis kuliner, online shop communities)
2. **First 2 weeks:** Only provide value. Answer questions, share tips. NO links.
3. **After building trust:** Share content naturally — "Btw, kemarin saya bikin video tentang [topic] di TikTok. Mungkin berguna: [link]"
4. **Max 1-2 shares per group per week**
5. **WA Status:** Share your TikTok/IG content 2-3x/week

### Post-Publish WA Action (Every Post)

1. Share to 3-5 WA groups within 5 min of posting
2. Ask 5-10 contacts to comment within 30 min
3. Seed comments should be genuine, 6+ words: "3 sih paling sering", "Semua 5 mau nangis"
