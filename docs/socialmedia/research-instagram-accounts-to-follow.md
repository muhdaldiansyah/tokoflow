## 1. How Instagram Algorithm Training Works

### How Following + Engagement Trains Your Feed (2026)

Instagram no longer uses one algorithm. It uses multiple AI-driven ranking systems across Feed, Stories, Reels, and Explore. Each surface has its own set of ranking signals.

**Key mechanisms:**

1. **Following signals niche identity.** When @catat.order follows accounts in the UMKM/bisnis/kuliner space, Instagram's AI begins categorizing your account into that interest cluster. Your Explore page and Suggested content will shift toward UMKM-related content.

2. **Engagement signals train recommendations.** Instagram tracks every like, comment, save, and share. Each interaction teaches the algorithm your preferences. Posts receiving lots of early engagement (likes, comments, shares, saves) are more likely to reach more people.

3. **Saves are the strongest signal.** Saves indicate "huge interest" to the algorithm. When you save posts from UMKM accounts, Instagram aggressively surfaces similar content.

4. **Shares trigger community mapping.** Instagram now heavily weights shares (DM sends). When you share UMKM content, the algorithm maps you into that community cluster.

5. **Watch time matters for Reels.** Instagram predicts whether you'll watch a Reel to the end. Watching 95%+ of a Reel is a massive signal. Watching UMKM/bisnis Reels to completion trains your algorithm fastest.

6. **Explore page uses ~36 ranking signals** focused on accounts you don't follow. It considers: your likelihood of following the creator, whether you'll view posts for 5+ seconds, and video completion rates.

### How This Benefits @catat.order

By consistently following and engaging with UMKM/bisnis accounts:
- Your content will be recommended to followers of those accounts
- Your Explore page shows competitor and industry content (research value)
- Instagram maps @catat.order into the "Indonesian UMKM SaaS" interest graph
- Your Reels get shown to users who engage with similar UMKM content

## 2. The 5-3-1 Daily Engagement Strategy

The 5-3-1 rule is a proven daily engagement framework for organic Instagram growth:

### The Rule
Every day:
- **Like 5 posts** from accounts in your niche
- **Comment on 3 posts** with thoughtful, relevant comments (not generic)
- **Follow 1 new account** in your niche

**Goal:** Establish niche identity

1. Follow all "MUST FOLLOW" accounts from every category (15-20 accounts)
2. Like 10 recent posts from each followed account
3. Save 3-5 posts that are most relevant to order management/UMKM
4. Comment thoughtfully on 5 posts per day
5. Watch all Reels from followed accounts to completion

### Phase 2: Expansion (Week 3-4)
**Goal:** Deepen algorithm training

1. Follow all "HIGH" priority accounts (15-20 more accounts)
2. Apply 5-3-1 rule daily
3. Start sharing/reposting relevant content to Stories
4. Join conversations in comments sections of followed accounts
5. Send relevant posts to @catat.order via DM (this trains the DM recommendation engine)

**Goal:** Build relationships and visibility

1. Follow "MEDIUM" priority accounts selectively
2. Start creating content that references/tags followed accounts
3. Collaborate with micro-UMKM accounts (1K-10K followers)
4. Engage with follower accounts of your followed accounts (their audience = your audience)
5. Track which account engagements drive the most profile visits
