## 1. Content Format Performance

### TikTok Format Rankings (2025-2026)

| Format | Reach | Engagement | Saves | Best For |
|--------|-------|------------|-------|----------|
| **Photo Carousels** | +3% vs video | +81% vs video | Highest | Education, tips, listicles |
| **Short Video (7-15s)** | Highest | Medium | Low | Hooks, trends, awareness |
| **Mid Video (21-34s)** | High | High | Medium | Product demos, storytelling |
| **Long Video (30-60s)** | Medium | Highest | High | Educational deep-dives |

**Key data points:**
- TikTok carousel photo posts get **2.9x more comments** and **1.9x more likes** than video posts (TikTok internal data)
- Photo posts generate **81% more engagements** and **82% more likes** than videos on TikTok
- TikTok rolled out a dedicated photo feed in early 2025 and added alt text to images, signaling strategic emphasis on static content
- The algorithm tracks how users interact with each carousel slide -- swipe-through behavior and time spent per image
- TikTok allows up to **35 images** in a carousel post
- Optimal carousel dimensions: **1080 x 1920 px** (9:16 aspect ratio)

### Instagram Format Rankings (2025-2026)

| Format | Reach | Engagement | Saves | Best For |
|--------|-------|------------|-------|----------|
| **Reels** | Highest (1.36x carousels) | Medium | Low | Discovery, new audiences |
| **Carousels** | High (1.4x single images) | Highest (+12% vs Reels) | Highest | Education, community |
| **Stories** | Low (followers only) | High (polls, Q&A) | N/A | Behind-scenes, engagement |
| **Static Posts** | Lowest | Lowest | Low | Brand aesthetic only |

**Key data points:**
- Reels get **2.25x more reach** than photos -- essential for accounts under 50K followers trying to grow
- Carousels get **12% more engagement** than Reels and **2.14x more** than single images
- Carousels generate **significantly more comments** than Reels, especially detailed/educational ones
- Carousels **dramatically outperform** Reels on saves (Instagram confirmed)
- Instagram allows up to **20 images/videos** in carousels (as of 2026)
- Optimal carousel dimensions: **1080 x 1350 px** (4:5 aspect ratio)

### CatatOrder Format Priority

| Platform | Primary Format | Secondary Format | Tertiary |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| **TikTok** | Photo carousels (education/tips) | Short video 15-30s (hooks, trends) | Mid video 30-60s (tutorials) |
| **Instagram** | Carousels (education/saves) | Reels (discovery/reach) | Stories (engagement/polls) |

**Rationale:** CatatOrder's value proposition is educational ("here's how to manage orders better"). Educational content performs best in carousel format on both platforms. Use video for reach/discovery, carousels for depth/saves.

## 2. Content Pillars for SaaS Targeting UMKM

### Recommended Pillar Framework

Based on 2025 B2B SaaS benchmarks, the optimal allocation is:

| Pillar | Allocation | Purpose | CatatOrder Examples |
|--------|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **Educational** | 40% | Authority + saves + trust | Tips orderan, tutorial WA bot, cara hitung profit |
| **Pain Point / Relatable** | 25% | Emotional hook + shares | "Chat pelanggan tenggelam", manual counting nightmares |
| **Social Proof** | 15% | Trust + conversion | User testimonials, before/after, growth stories |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | 10% | Humanize brand + connection | Building CatatOrder, founder story, day-in-life |
| **Promotional** | 10% | Direct conversion | Feature showcase, free signup CTA, comparison posts |

### Detailed Pillar Breakdown

#### Pillar 1: Educational (40%) -- "Guru UMKM"

Content types:
- **Tips listicles:** "5 Cara Atur Orderan WA Biar Gak Ketuker"
- **How-to tutorials:** "Cara Kirim Struk Digital ke Pelanggan dalam 30 Detik"
- **Mistake posts:** "Kesalahan Fatal UMKM yang Bikin Profit Bocor" (like Post 1)
- **Data/insight:** "Rata-rata UMKM kehilangan Rp2jt/bulan karena salah catat"
- **Mini-course series:** "Kelas Orderan" 7-day series

Why 40%: Educational content gets the most saves on both platforms. Saves = algorithm boost = more reach. UMKM owners actively seek practical knowledge.

#### Pillar 2: Pain Point / Relatable (25%) -- "Aku Juga Gitu"

Content types:
- **"POV" format:** "POV: pelanggan ke-47 nanya 'udah dikirim belum kak?'"
- **Before/after:** "Dulu catat di kertas, sekarang..."
- **Meme-style:** Business owner chaos moments
- **Storytelling:** "Cerita UMKM yang kehilangan Rp5jt gara-gara salah catat"
- **"Yang jualan via WA pasti paham" hooks**

Why 25%: Emotional resonance drives shares and comments. Indonesian UMKM owners love content that validates their struggles. This is the "hook" pillar that stops the scroll.

#### Pillar 3: Social Proof (15%) -- "Bukti Nyata"

Content types:
- **User screenshots:** Dashboard showing organized orders
- **Testimonial quotes:** "Dulu 2 jam ngitung, sekarang 5 menit"
- **Numbers/stats:** "150 orderan tercatat rapi bulan ini"
- **Case study mini-stories:** Specific UMKM transformation
- **Community highlights:** Repost user content

Why 15%: Trust-building is essential for SaaS conversion in Indonesia. UMKM owners are skeptical of new tools -- social proof reduces friction.

#### Pillar 4: Behind-the-Scenes (10%) -- "Di Balik Layar"

Content types:
- **Founder journey:** "Kenapa bikin CatatOrder"
- **Building in public:** Bug fixes, new features, user feedback response
- **Day-in-life:** Solo developer/founder routine
- **Product development:** "Lagi bikin fitur baru nih..."
- **Values/mission:** Why UMKM digital transformation matters

Why 10%: Humanizes the brand. Indonesian audiences connect strongly with founders/people behind products. Builds loyalty and emotional investment.

#### Pillar 5: Promotional (10%) -- "Cobain Sekarang"

Content types:
- **Feature demos:** Screen recording of CatatOrder in action
- **Comparison:** "Manual vs CatatOrder" side-by-side
- **Free offer:** "Gratis 150 orderan/bulan, gak perlu kartu kredit"
- **CTA posts:** Direct signup invitation with clear benefit
- **New feature announcements**

Why 10%: Keep promotional content minimal to avoid "jualan terus" perception. Only promote after building trust through other pillars.

3. **Save-worthy** (reference material they'll come back to)
4. **Share-worthy to WA** (makes them look smart in WA groups)

## 4. Bahasa Indonesia Copywriting Guide

### The "Bahasa Gaul Profesional" Spectrum

Indonesian social media copywriting sits on a spectrum from formal to ultra-casual. For CatatOrder targeting UMKM owners (typically 25-45 years old), the sweet spot is **"professional casual"** -- friendly, relatable, but not childish.

### Tone Positioning

| Level | Example | Use For |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Formal** | "Kelola pesanan Anda dengan efisien" | Legal pages, terms & conditions |
| **Professional** | "Kelola orderan bisnis kamu lebih rapi" | Website, product descriptions |
| **Bahasa Gaul Profesional** (TARGET) | "Orderan berantakan? Ada caranya biar rapi" | Social media captions, TikTok |
| **Bahasa Gaul** | "Goks sih ini, orderan auto rapi" | Very casual replies, memes |
| **Alay/Slang** | "Cuzz cobain, mantul banget" | AVOID -- too young, not credible |

### Core Copywriting Rules

**1. Use "kamu" not "Anda" or "kami"**
- Good: "Orderan kamu gak akan ketuker lagi"
- Avoid: "Kelola pesanan Anda dengan mudah"
- Exception: legal/privacy pages use "kami"

**2. Mix Indonesian with common abbreviations**
- "gak" instead of "tidak"
- "udah" instead of "sudah"
- "biar" instead of "agar"
- "aja" instead of "saja"
- "banget" instead of "sangat"
- "tuh" instead of "itu"
- "dong" for emphasis
- "sih" for softening
- "deh" for suggestion

**3. Use UMKM-specific vocabulary**
- "orderan" (not "pesanan" on social media)
- "pelanggan" (not "konsumen")
- "omzet" (not "pendapatan")
- "DP" (everyone knows this)
- "chat WA" (not "pesan WhatsApp")
- "struk" (not "kwitansi" or "tanda terima")
- "jualan" (not "berjualan")
- "modal" (not "investasi awal")
- "untung" / "profit" (both work)
- "boncos" (losing money -- very relatable slang)
- "cuan" (profit -- trendy but use sparingly)

**4. Emotional triggers in Bahasa**
- "Capek gak sih..." (exhaustion validation)
- "Yang jualan via WA pasti paham..." (inclusion)
- "Beneran deh..." (authenticity)
- "Nah ini nih..." (revelation)
- "Jangan-jangan kamu masih..." (guilt/curiosity)
- "Ternyata..." (surprise insight)
- "Padahal..." (contrast/revelation)
- "Cuma butuh..." (simplicity promise)

**5. Hashtag language**
- Use Indonesian hashtags primarily
- Mix: #tipsumkm #jualanonline #orderwa #umkmindonesia
- Branded: #catatorder
- Avoid: #fyp #foryoupage (confirmed myth, wastes hashtag slots)
- Max 5 hashtags on TikTok (restriction since Aug 2025)

### Caption Structure Template

```
[Hook -- 1 line, max 10 words] + emoji

[Problem statement -- 1-2 lines, relatable]

[Solution/content promise -- 1 line]

[CTA -- question or action] + emoji

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3 #hashtag4 #branded
```

**Example for CatatOrder:**
```
Yang jualan via WA, cek dulu sebelum rugi lagi

Kesalahan kecil tapi bikin orderan berantakan dan profit bocor.

Yang mana yang paling sering kamu lakuin? Komen nomornya!

#tipsumkm #orderwa #jualanonline #umkm #catatorder
```

### Words/Phrases to AVOID

| Avoid | Why | Use Instead |
|-------|-----|-------------|
| "Kami" | Too corporate | Product name or "kamu" |
| "Solusi terdepan" | Overused marketing speak | Describe specific benefit |
| "Revolusioner" | Cringe for UMKM | "Gampang banget" |
| "Gratis selamanya" | Unbelievable | "Gratis 150 orderan/bulan" |
| English jargon | Alienating | Indonesian equivalent |
| "Download sekarang" | Pushy | "Coba gratis aja dulu" |
| Excessive emojis | Looks spammy | Max 2-3 per caption |

## 5. Viral Content Patterns on Indonesian Business TikTok

### Hook Formulas That Work

**Critical stat:** 71% of TikTok users decide whether to continue watching within the first 3 seconds. Videos with strong 3-second retention (above 65%) receive **4-7x more impressions**.

#### Top-Performing Hook Types for Business Content

**1. Problem-Agitation Hook (Highest performer for UMKM)**
```
"Yang jualan via WA, jangan lakuin ini..."
"UMKM yang masih catat orderan di kertas, awas..."
"5 kesalahan yang bikin omzet bocor"
```

**2. Curiosity Gap Hook**
```
"Ternyata ini alasan orderan kamu sering ketuker"
"1 hal simpel yang bedain UMKM sukses dan gagal"
"Rahasia UMKM omzet 100jt/bulan yang jarang diomongin"
```

**3. Contrarian/Bold Statement Hook**
```
"Catat orderan di buku tulis itu buang waktu"
"UMKM gak butuh aplikasi kasir"
"Stop balas 'siap kak' tanpa dicatat"
```

**4. "POV" Hook (Highly relatable)**
```
"POV: pelanggan ke-50 nanya 'pesanan saya mana kak'"
"POV: ngitung omzet jam 11 malem pake kalkulator"
"POV: chat pelanggan udah tenggelam di WA"
```

**5. Number/Listicle Hook**
```
"5 kesalahan fatal UMKM di WA"
"3 cara atur orderan biar gak berantakan"
"1 tool gratis yang bisa hemat 2 jam sehari"
```

**6. "Dulu vs Sekarang" Hook**
```
"Dulu catat orderan di kertas... sekarang..."
"6 bulan lalu orderan masih berantakan, sekarang..."
```

### Visual Hook Best Practices

- **On-screen text overlay** in the first frame -- reinforces verbal hook, captures sound-off viewers
- **Face on camera** in first 2 seconds -- human faces get 2.5x more engagement
- **Movement/action** in first frame -- static opener = scroll past
- **Bright colors / high contrast** -- stands out in feed
- **Text size:** Large, bold, max 2 lines visible

| Topic Category | Engagement Level | CatatOrder Angle |

### Viral Content Patterns

1. **"Ternyata" revelations** -- exposing something UMKM owners didn't know they were doing wrong
2. **Before/after transformations** -- messy desk/WA chat --> organized dashboard
3. **Relatable chaos** -- showing the actual mess of managing orders manually (then offering solution)
4. **"Ngapain ribet" simplification** -- showing a simpler way to do something common
5. **Duet/stitch format** -- responding to other UMKM creators' pain point content
6. **Series content** -- "Hari ke-1 pakai CatatOrder" (day 1, day 7, day 30 updates)

## 6. Growth Playbook: 0 to 10K Followers

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2) -- Target: 0 to 200 followers

**Account Setup:**
- Complete profile: logo, bio with clear value prop, website link (catatorder.id)
- Bio formula: `[What you help with] | [For whom] | [Free CTA]`
- Example: "Atur orderan WA jadi rapi | Untuk UMKM Indonesia | Gratis di catatorder.id"
- Switch to Business Account (unlocks analytics)

**Warm-up Protocol (CRITICAL for new accounts):**
- Days 1-3: Post 1 content/day, spend 30 min engaging with UMKM content
- Days 4-7: Post 1-2 content/day, comment on 20+ related posts daily
- Engage genuinely -- not "Nice!" but meaningful 5+ word comments
- Follow 10-20 relevant UMKM accounts per day
- Like and save competitor content (trains algorithm to understand your niche)

**Content Strategy:**
- Start with carousels (easier to produce, higher engagement on TikTok)
- Focus entirely on pain point and educational content
- Repurpose same carousel across TikTok + Instagram
- Cross-post to relevant WA groups after each post

### Phase 2: Traction (Week 3-6) -- Target: 200 to 1,000 followers

**Content Cadence:**
- TikTok: 5-7 posts/week (mix carousels + short video)
- Instagram: 4-5 posts/week (carousels + Reels) + daily Stories
- Batch create content on weekends for the week ahead

**Growth Tactics:**
- Identify 5-10 trending sounds weekly, create content using them
- Respond to EVERY comment within the first hour (doubles comment count)
- Stitch/duet other UMKM creators' content
- Share every post to 3-5 WA groups within 5 minutes of posting
- Seed 5-10 genuine comments from contacts within first 30 min
- Pin the best comment on each post

**Content Experiments:**
- Test different hook types (problem, curiosity, POV, number)
- Track which carousel topics get most saves
- Note: TikTok algorithm doesn't prioritize follower count -- any video can go viral
- Analyze which posts drive profile visits (these are converting content)

### Phase 3: Momentum (Week 7-12) -- Target: 1,000 to 5,000 followers

**Algorithm Optimization:**
- Double down on content types that got 2x+ average engagement
- Create series content (numbered parts drive follow-for-more behavior)
- Use analytics to identify exact peak times for YOUR audience
- Start introducing social proof content (user testimonials, screenshots)

**Community Building:**
- Reply with video responses to top comments
- Create "requested" content based on comment themes
- Start WA community group for followers
- Feature user stories (even if few users -- one strong story is enough)

**Collaboration:**
- Comment on and engage with Indonesian SaaS/business creator content
- Propose carousel collabs with complementary UMKM accounts
- Tag relevant accounts in relatable content

### Phase 4: Scale (Month 4-6) -- Target: 5,000 to 10,000 followers

**Content Maturity:**
- Establish recognizable content format/style
- Create content "franchises" (recurring series with loyal audience)
- Introduce more promotional content (now that trust is built)
- Start live sessions (Q&A, demo, casual chat about UMKM challenges)

**Cross-Platform:**
- YouTube Shorts repurpose from TikTok
- LinkedIn posts for B2B angle
- Blog/SEO content linked from social

**Milestone Tactics:**
- "Road to 10K" transparency content
- Follower milestone celebrations with giveaways/freebies
- Micro-influencer collaborations (barter, not paid)

### Realistic Timeline

| Milestone | Optimistic | Realistic | Conservative |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| 100 followers | Week 1 | Week 2 | Week 3 |
| 500 followers | Week 3 | Week 5 | Week 8 |
| 1,000 followers | Week 5 | Week 8 | Week 12 |
| 5,000 followers | Month 3 | Month 5 | Month 8 |
| 10,000 followers | Month 5 | Month 8 | Month 12 |

**Variables that accelerate:** Consistent daily posting, 1+ viral post (10K+ views), active community engagement, trending sound usage, collaboration with larger accounts.

## 8. UGC and Community Building

### UGC Strategy for CatatOrder

#### Phase 1: Seed UGC (0-1K followers)

Since CatatOrder has limited users, "UGC" must be manufactured authentically:

1. **Self-created case studies:** Use your own or friends' businesses as demo accounts. Show real dashboards, real order flows.

2. **Screenshot testimonials:** Even 1 user saying "Ini gampang banget" is usable content. Ask existing user for WhatsApp feedback screenshots.

3. **Before/after content:** Create side-by-side comparisons: messy WhatsApp chat vs organized CatatOrder dashboard.

4. **"Beta tester" program:** Recruit 10-20 UMKM owners to try CatatOrder free. Ask them to:
   - Screenshot their dashboard after 1 week
   - Record a 15-second voice message about their experience
   - Share their honest feedback (even criticism = content)

#### Phase 2: Encourage Organic UGC (1K-5K followers)

1. **Make sharing effortless:**
   - Add "Share to Story" prompts in the app after milestones (first order, 100th order)
   - WA branding footer already drives passive discovery

2. **Create share-worthy moments:**
   - Monthly recap that users want to screenshot and share
   - Achievement badges ("100 orderan tercatat!")
   - Clean dashboard screenshots that look good on Stories

3. **UGC hashtag campaign:**
   - #CatatOrderku -- users share their setup
   - #OrderanRapi -- broader community tag
   - Feature best posts on CatatOrder's account

4. **Comment-to-content pipeline:**
   - Screenshot relatable comments from your posts
   - Create new content responding to them
   - "Banyak yang nanya soal ini, jadi bikin video khusus"

#### Phase 3: Community Building (5K+ followers)

1. **WA Community Group:**
   - Create a WA group for UMKM owners (not just CatatOrder users)
   - Share tips, answer questions, build relationships
   - Members become organic ambassadors

2. **Instagram Close Friends / Broadcast Channel:**
   - Exclusive tips for engaged followers
   - Early access to new features
   - Behind-the-scenes content

3. **Monthly challenges:**
   - "Challenge: Catat semua orderan selama 1 minggu"
   - Participants share screenshots, tag @catat.order
   - Winner gets featured + free upgrade

### Community-First Content Ideas

| Type | Format | Example |
|------|--------|---------|

### The Indonesian UMKM Conversion Funnel

### Optimizing Each Step

#### Step 1: Content to Profile Visit

- Every post should give a reason to check the profile (not just consume and scroll)
- **Soft CTA in content:** "Mau tau caranya? Cek profil"
- **Series content:** "Part 1 of 5 -- follow biar gak ketinggalan"
- **Tease value in bio:** "Template orderan gratis di bio"
- AVOID: "Link di bio" in caption/comments (algorithm deprioritizes, looks spammy)

#### Step 2: Profile to Bio Link Click

**Bio optimization:**
```
Atur orderan WA jadi rapi
Untuk UMKM Indonesia
Gratis 150 orderan/bulan
catatorder.id
```

- Clear value prop in first line
- Target audience stated
- Free offer prominently shown
- Single link (no link-in-bio tool needed -- direct to catatorder.id)
- Profile picture: CatatOrder logo (consistent with brand)

**Link in bio best practices:**
- Use UTM parameters: `catatorder.id/?utm_source=tiktok&utm_medium=bio`
- Track clicks separately from organic traffic
- CTA hierarchy: prevent decision paralysis -- one clear primary action
- Email capture: Consider adding email signup with incentive (free template, checklist)

#### Step 3: Landing Page to Signup

CatatOrder's landing page already has:
- Pain-point hook with interactive demo
- 3-step "how it works"
- Single green CTA ("Mulai Gratis")
- Google OAuth (frictionless signup)

**Optimization for social traffic:**
- Mobile-first experience (100% of social traffic is mobile)
- Page load speed under 3 seconds
- CTA above the fold
- Social proof element visible immediately (even "1 UMKM sudah pakai" is better than nothing)

#### Step 4: Signup to Activation

- Current onboarding checklist (3 steps) is good
- First-order celebration bottom sheet drives WA send
- WA branding footer creates viral loop

### Content-Specific CTA Strategies

| Content Type | CTA Approach |
|-------------|-------------|
| Educational carousel | Last slide: "Mau lebih gampang? Ada caranya" (soft) |
| Pain point video | Comment reply: "Gue bikin tool buat ini, gratis" |
| Social proof | Show the dashboard, let curiosity drive profile visit |
| Tutorial | "Step lengkapnya ada di catatorder.id" (natural) |
| Behind-the-scenes | No CTA -- pure relationship building |

### Conversion Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Tool |
|--------|--------|------|
| Profile visits per post | 2-5% of views | TikTok/IG Analytics |
| Bio link CTR | 3-5% of profile visits | UTM + Vercel Analytics |
| Signup rate | 10-20% of landing page visitors | Supabase Auth |
| Activation rate | 50%+ create first order | Supabase profiles |
| WA send rate | 30%+ send first WA | first_wa_sent_at tracking |

### Key Rule: Never Be Pushy

Indonesian UMKM owners are highly sensitive to being "sold to." The conversion strategy should feel like:
- "Oh, ada tool gratis yang bisa bantu, kenapa gak coba"
- NOT "Download sekarang! Promo terbatas!"

Build value first (80% of content), convert naturally (20% of content).

## 10. Trending Sounds/Music Strategy

### How to Find Trending Sounds

#### TikTok

1. **Creative Center:** `ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/inspiration/popular/music/pc/en`
   - Filter by region (Indonesia) and industry
   - Shows trending sounds with usage data

2. **Discover Page:** Browse "Trending" tab for current viral sounds
   - Check `tiktok.com/discover/sound-yang-lagi-viral-langsung-fyp`
   - Check `tiktok.com/discover/trending-tiktok-sounds-indonesia`

3. **Commercial Sounds Library:**
   - When creating a post, tap "Add sound" --> "Commercial sounds"
   - These are pre-cleared for business accounts (no copyright issues)
   - Check `tiktok.com/discover/sound-komersial-trending-2025`

4. **For You Page observation:**
   - When you see the same sound 3+ times in 10 minutes of scrolling, it's trending
   - Save it immediately for use within 24-48 hours

5. **Creator search:** Search "sound viral tiktok 2025" on TikTok itself
   - Many creators compile trending sound lists weekly

#### Instagram

1. **Reels tab:** Browse Reels and note sounds with the arrow icon (trending indicator)
2. **Audio page:** Tap any sound to see usage count
3. **Explore page:** Note recurring audio in recommended Reels

### Sound Usage Strategy for Business Accounts

**Rule 1: Use trending sounds within 48 hours of spotting them**
- Trending sounds have a lifecycle: emerging (48h) --> peak (3-5 days) --> declining (1 week)
- Best to catch them in the emerging phase
- Using a sound at peak still works but less algorithmic boost

**Rule 2: Match sound energy to content type**

| Content Type | Sound Type | Example |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Educational carousel | Calm/chill background | Lo-fi, soft instrumentals |
| Pain point hook | Dramatic/tension | Dramatic reveal sounds |
| Transformation/before-after | Build-up music | Songs with beat drop |
| Relatable/humor | Trending viral sound | Whatever is viral this week |
| Motivational | Uplifting/inspirational | Popular Indonesian songs |

**Rule 3: Business accounts use "commercial sounds"**
- Business accounts on TikTok have access to commercial sound library
- These won't cause copyright strikes
- Search: "commercial sounds trending" in TikTok

**Rule 4: Indonesian sounds perform better for Indonesian audience**
- Dangdut koplo trending sounds get massive engagement in Indonesia
- Indonesian pop songs in trending create local relevance
- Avoid exclusively using Western sounds

**Currently Trending Indonesian Sounds (Feb 2026):**
- Various dangdut koplo remixes
- "DJ Kau Tigakan Cintaku" variations
- "Aku Dah Lupa" edits

- Higher save rate = algorithmic boost

**Why Reels matter for small accounts on IG:**
- For accounts under 50K followers, Reels drive highest reach

- Essential for discovery and breaking out of existing follower bubble

## 12. Posting Frequency and Calendar

### Optimal Posting Frequency
