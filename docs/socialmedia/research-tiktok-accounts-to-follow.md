## Algorithm Training Strategy

### How TikTok Algorithm Training Works

TikTok's algorithm learns from your account's behavior: what you watch, like, comment on, share, and who you follow. For a **brand account** like @catat.order, the algorithm uses these signals to:

1. **Categorize your account** into interest clusters (e.g., "UMKM Indonesia", "bisnis kuliner", "manajemen pesanan")
2. **Distribute your content** to users in those same clusters
3. **Surface relevant content** on your FYP so you stay informed about trends in your niche

### Key Principles

| Principle | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Follow niche creators with 10K-100K followers** | They spot trends early, their followers overlap with your target audience. More valuable than mega-influencers. |
| **Engage meaningfully** | Spend 20 min/day commenting on 10-15 creators in your exact niche. Comments should be genuine and thoughtful. |
| **Watch full videos** | Completion rate is a strong signal. Watch UMKM/bisnis content fully. Skip unrelated content quickly. |
| **Like and share strategically** | Like content you want the algorithm to associate with your account. Share to WhatsApp (strong signal). |
| **Post consistently** | 3-5 times per week minimum. The algorithm favors active accounts. |

### Ideal Follower-to-Following Ratio

TikTok does NOT penalize brand accounts for following many accounts the way Instagram does. However:

- **New account phase (0-1K followers):** Following 200-500 accounts is fine. Focus on quality follows.
- **Growth phase (1K-10K):** Aim for a 1:3 to 1:5 ratio (followers : following). Following 300-500 accounts is normal.
- **Established phase (10K+):** Gradually unfollow non-engaging accounts. Target 1:1 or higher ratio.
- **Key insight:** On TikTok, content quality matters far more than follower ratios. A brand new account with zero followers can reach millions if the content resonates.

### How Many Accounts to Follow Per Category

| Category | Recommended Follows | Reason |
|----------|-------------------|--------|
| Business Education (Large) | 8-10 | Algorithm signal; these are the "anchor" accounts for the niche |
| Business Education (Mid-Tier) | 10-15 | Highest engagement value; their followers are your target |
| SaaS/Tech Brands | 5-8 | Complementary positioning; competitor awareness |
| Food/Culinary Business | 8-10 | Direct target market (UMKM kuliner) |
| Online Selling/Marketing | 5-8 | Adjacent niche; helps with algorithm clustering |
| Government/Institutional | 3-5 | Legitimacy signal; UMKM policy content |
| Small UMKM/Micro-Creators | 15-20 | Most valuable for algorithm training; genuine UMKM owners |
| **Total** | **54-76** | Sweet spot for first month |

### Engagement Protocol (Daily, 20 minutes)

1. Open TikTok, scroll FYP for 5 min -- watch UMKM/bisnis content fully, skip non-relevant quickly
2. Visit 3-5 followed accounts, watch their latest video, leave a genuine comment
3. Like 10-15 posts from UMKM-related content
4. Share 1-2 posts to WhatsApp (strong algorithm signal)
5. Search for hashtags: #UMKM, #BisnisKuliner, #TipsUMKM, #UsahaKecil, #JualanOnline, #OrderManagement

| Type | What to Look For | Why Valuable |
|------|-----------------|--------------|

| Home-based food sellers | Behind-the-scenes cooking, packing, shipping | They need order management badly |
| Catering businesses | Event prep, order management challenges | Core CatatOrder use case |
| Coffee shop owners | Daily cafe operations, menu management | F&B management audience |
