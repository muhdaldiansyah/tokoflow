## 1. How the TikTok Algorithm Works in 2026

### Core Ranking Signals (Hierarchy)

The algorithm evaluates thousands of signals per video. Here is the weighted hierarchy based on leaked/reverse-engineered data:

**Viral Scoring System (Weighted Points per interaction):**

| Signal | Points | Weight |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Retention Rate (avg watch time) | 50 pts | HIGHEST |
| Completion Rate (watched to end) | 30 pts | Very High |
| Rewatch Rate (looping) | 20 pts | High |
| Shares (especially DM/dark social) | 15 pts | High |
| Saves (Favorites) | 10 pts | High |
| Comments | 5 pts | Medium |
| Likes | 1 pt | LOW |

**Alternative weighted formula used by analytics platforms:**
`((Likes x 1) + (Comments x 5) + (Shares x 7) + (Saves x 10)) / Views x 100`

**Key takeaway:** Likes are nearly worthless. Saves and shares dominate. Watch time is king.

### The Four Signal Tiers

| Tier | Category | What It Includes | Weight |
|------|----------|-----------------|--------|
| 1 | Retention signals | Watch time, completion rate, rewatch loops | Highest |
| 2 | Active engagement | Shares, saves, comments, follows-from-video | High |
| 3 | Content/SEO signals | Keywords, hashtags, on-screen text, audio | Medium |
| 4 | Passive signals | Likes, profile views, scroll-past speed | Lower |

### FYP Distribution: Follower-First Testing (MAJOR 2026 CHANGE)

This is the single biggest algorithm change in 2025-2026:

1. **When you upload a video, it is shown primarily to your EXISTING FOLLOWERS first** (not random non-followers like before)
2. TikTok analyzes performance among your followers: completion rate, likes, comments, shares, saves
3. **Only after this evaluation** does the algorithm decide whether to push to non-followers
4. Your followers are effectively a "test panel" for content quality

**Critical implication for 0-follower accounts:** Since you have no followers to test with, TikTok falls back to the old system -- showing to ~200-300 random users matched by topic/interest signals. This means your FIRST followers matter enormously because they become your test panel.

### What the Algorithm Scans

- **Video content:** Visual recognition, object detection, scene analysis
- **Audio:** Speech-to-text transcription, music identification, original audio detection
- **Text:** Caption keywords, on-screen text (OCR), hashtags
- **User behavior:** Watch time, scroll speed, replays, shares, saves
- **Account signals:** Posting consistency, niche focus, engagement history

## 2. New Account Algorithm Training (First 7-14 Days)

### How TikTok Learns Your Account

- The algorithm starts learning from your **first video**
- It typically takes **5-10 posts** before TikTok accurately categorizes your content niche
- During first 7-14 days, your content is tested via the **old system** (random 200-300 user test batches) since you have no followers yet

### First 7 Days Protocol

1. **Post 1x per day minimum** -- consistency signals to the algorithm you are a serious creator
2. **Pick ONE niche and stick to it** -- TikTok needs to categorize you. Random topics confuse the algorithm
3. **Spend 15-30 minutes daily** watching and engaging with content in your niche (this trains your account's interest graph)
4. **Leave thoughtful comments** on videos in your vertical -- this exposes your profile to potential followers
5. **Use the same 3-5 niche hashtags** consistently to reinforce topic categorization
6. **Do NOT mass follow/unfollow** -- this triggers spam detection immediately on new accounts
7. **Do NOT post more than 3x per day** in the first week -- new accounts posting aggressively get flagged

### First 14 Days Targets

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Posts published | 10-14 posts | 1/day minimum to train algorithm |
| Average completion rate | 60%+ | Proves content quality |
| Niche consistency | 100% same topic | Helps algorithm categorize you |
| Engagement ratio | 5%+ engagement rate | Shows content resonates |
| Hook retention (3s) | 70%+ viewers stay past 3s | Critical for initial test |

### What Matters MOST for New Accounts

1. **Completion rate** -- more than anything else, this determines if your video escapes the 200-view test batch
2. **Hook strength** -- 71% of users decide within **3 seconds** whether to keep watching
3. **Niche clarity** -- the algorithm must figure out WHO to show your content to
4. **Posting consistency** -- accounts that post sporadically get deprioritized
5. **"Qualified Views"** -- views must last **5+ seconds** to count as quality views in 2026

### Escaping "200 View Jail"

- Videos must accumulate at least **50 engagement points** from the initial 200-300 test viewers within **first 2-4 hours**
- You need **12-15 consistent high-performing videos** over 3-6 weeks to permanently escape
- Fastest escape: post **5-15 second videos** with strong 3-second hooks (easier to get high completion rates)

## 3. Carousel/Photo Post Algorithm Signals

### Why Carousels Matter for CatatOrder

Carousels are IDEAL for business/educational content because:

- **81% higher engagement rate** than video content
- **2x more comments** than short-form videos
- **3x more saves** than short-form videos
- Only **1/3 fewer shares** than video (acceptable tradeoff given the save/comment boost)
- Average engagement rate: **4.90%** (vs platform average of 2.50%)

### Key Algorithm Signals for Carousels

| Signal | How It Works | Weight |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Swipe-through rate** | % of users who swipe through ALL slides. Acts like video completion rate. | HIGHEST |
| **Dwell time per slide** | How long users linger on each image. Signals interest quality. | Very High |
| **Slide completion rate** | % of slides viewed out of total. 100% = swiped through all. | Very High |
| **Saves** | Carousels drive 3x more saves than video -- algorithm interprets as high-value. | High |
| **Comments** | 2x more comments = strong engagement signal to algorithm. | High |
| **Return visits** | Users coming back to re-view carousel = very strong signal. | High |

### Optimal Carousel Configuration

| Parameter | Recommendation | Reason |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Number of slides | **5-10 slides** (sweet spot: 7) | Enough for dwell time, not so many users drop off |
| Image dimensions | **1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)** | Native format, no letterboxing |
| Text per slide | **Concise, large fonts** | Too much text = quick swipe (low dwell time) |
| First slide | **Hook/title slide** | Determines if users start swiping at all |
| Last slide | **CTA slide** | "Save for later" / "Follow for more" / question prompt |

### Carousel Best Practices for CatatOrder

1. **First slide is your hook** -- treat it like a video thumbnail. Must be visually captivating and clearly indicate value
2. **Each slide must add value** -- algorithm tracks per-slide dwell time
3. **Use text overlays with keywords** -- TikTok OCR reads text on carousel images for SEO indexing
4. **End with a question or CTA** -- drives comments (5 pts each)
5. **Include "save-worthy" information** -- tips, checklists, data that people want to reference later (saves = 10 pts each)
6. **Add trending or relevant audio** -- even for carousels, audio selection matters

## 4. Content Distribution Phases

### Phase 1: Initial Test (0-3 hours)

- Video shown to **200-500 viewers** (mix of followers + topic-matched non-followers)
- For accounts with followers: **followers get priority** in this test batch
- For 0-follower accounts: **200-300 random topic-matched users**
- Algorithm evaluates: completion rate, engagement velocity, qualified views

**Threshold to pass:** ~40%+ completion rate AND ~50 engagement points in 2-4 hours

### Phase 2: Expanded Test (~1,000 views)

- If Phase 1 metrics are strong, video pushed to **~1,000 users**
- Broader audience, still topic-matched
- Required metrics: **50%+ completion rate, 10%+ engagement rate**
- Algorithm evaluates same signals with larger sample size

### Phase 3: Wider Distribution (~2,000-10,000 views)

- If Phase 2 passes, video enters wider FYP distribution
- Pushed to **2,000-10,000+ users** in waves
- Each wave tests same metrics -- as long as they stay high, waves continue
- This is where you start seeing rapid view count acceleration

### Phase 4: Viral Push (10,000+ views)

- Strong metrics through Phase 3 trigger **viral distribution**
- Video pushed to **hundreds of thousands** of users
- Each new wave performs its own test -- engagement must sustain
- **Engagement velocity matters:** 100 shares in first hour = 10x more valuable than 100 shares over a week

### Phase 5: Plateau/Decline

- Distribution slows as algorithm exhausts relevant audience segments
- Engagement rates naturally decline with broader audiences
- Video may get "second wind" days/weeks later if rediscovered

### Critical 15-Minute Window

- Videos must hit specific **weighted engagement thresholds within the first 15 minutes** to reach broader audiences
- This means: post when your audience is MOST active, and engage immediately with early comments

### Viral Thresholds (2026)

| Metric | Threshold | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Completion rate | **70%+** | Up from 50% in 2024 |
| Qualified views (5s+) | High % of total views | New metric in 2026 |
| Rewatch rate | **15-20%** | One viewer rewatching 3x beats 3 viewers watching 1x |
| Engagement velocity | High within **first hour** | Speed matters more than total count |
| Short videos (<15s) | **100%+ completion** | Must indicate rewatching/looping |

## 6. Hashtag Strategy 2026

### How Many Hashtags

- **Official TikTok limit: 5 hashtags per post** (enforced since 2025)
- **Optimal: 3-5 hashtags** (confirmed by CapCut/ByteDance data)
- Using more than 5 is no longer possible

### Does #FYP Work?

**NO.** Using #fyp, #viral, #foryou, #foryoupage:
- Does NOT place you on FYP -- that is determined by engagement metrics
- Is extremely **saturated** (billions of posts) -- provides zero competitive advantage
- Does NOT help the algorithm categorize your content
- May actually **dilute** your topic signals by being too generic
- Wastes one of your 5 hashtag slots

### The 2026 Hashtag Formula

Use this mix of 3-5 hashtags:

| Type | Count | Example for CatatOrder | Volume |
|------|-------|----------------------|--------|
| **Niche-specific** | 2 | #catatorder #pencatatanpesanan | <10K posts |
| **Medium vertical** | 1-2 | #UMKM #bisniskuliner #jualanonline | 10K-100K posts |
| **Trending/seasonal** | 0-1 | (only if genuinely relevant) | 100K+ posts |

**Niche hashtags deliver 60-70% higher engagement** than broad hashtags because you compete in a smaller pool.

### Keywords > Hashtags in 2026

The biggest shift: **keywords in captions now outrank hashtags** for discoverability. The algorithm reads and indexes:
1. Caption text (first 150 characters weighted most heavily)
2. On-screen text (OCR)
3. Spoken words (speech-to-text)
4. Hashtags (now supplementary, not primary)

**System A (Leaked internal, per Joyspace/beatstorapon):**

| Action | Points | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rewatch/Loop | 5 pts | Strongest retention signal |
| Watch to completion | 4 pts | Core metric |
| Share | 3 pts | Distribution intent signal |
| Comment | 2 pts | Active engagement |
| Like | 1 pt | Weakest signal |

**System B (Analytics-weighted formula):**

| Action | Multiplier | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Save | x10 | "Future intent" -- strongest value signal |
| Share | x7 | Distribution/recommendation signal |
| Comment | x5 | Active engagement |
| Like | x1 | Baseline acknowledgment |

### What This Means in Practice

1. **1 save = 10 likes** in algorithmic weight
2. **1 share = 7 likes** in algorithmic weight
3. **1 comment = 5 likes** in algorithmic weight
4. **Comment QUALITY matters** -- lengthy, substantive comments weigh more than "nice!" or emoji-only
5. Likes are essentially vanity metrics -- they have minimal impact on distribution

### How to Engineer High-Value Engagement

| Target Action | Tactic | Points Earned |

## 8. What Kills Reach -- Shadowban Triggers

### Confirmed Shadowban Triggers

**Content Violations:**
- Profanity and explicit language
- Sexually suggestive content
- Violence or dangerous acts
- Unverified health/political/news claims (misinformation)
- Copyright violations (especially strict for business accounts in 2026)
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Using banned/flagged hashtags (even accidentally)

**Spam Behavior (Most Relevant for New Accounts):**

| Action | Risk Level | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| 3-5 uploads/day spaced out | SAFE | Normal |
| 6-10 uploads/day | INCREASED RISK | Yellow zone |
| 10+ uploads/day or multiple within minutes | LIKELY TRIGGER | Red zone |
| Mass following/unfollowing | HIGH RISK | Even small amounts on new accounts |
| Copy-pasting same comment repeatedly | HIGH RISK | Detected as bot behavior |
| Using third-party automation tools | HIGH RISK | TikTok actively detects these |
| Buying likes/comments/followers | EXTREME RISK | Can result in permanent ban |

**Account Activity Red Flags:**
- Dormant account suddenly posting daily (triggers review)
- Rapid shifts in IP address/device/location
- Managing multiple accounts from same device without segregation
- Sudden dramatic change in content topic

**Technical Red Flags:**
- Watermarks from other platforms (Instagram, YouTube logos)
- Low-resolution/blurry content
- Duplicate content (reposting same video)
- Using sounds that are flagged/removed

### Shadowban Duration

- Typical: **7-14 days** (two weeks)
- Serious violations: **Several weeks to months**
- Recovery steps: Delete violating content, stop posting for 2-3 days, resume with clean content

### What NOT to Do (CatatOrder Specific)

1. Do NOT use trending audio you do not have rights to (business accounts are restricted)
2. Do NOT post more than 3x per day in the first 2 weeks
3. Do NOT include competitor brand names or logos
4. Do NOT use engagement bait that TikTok specifically penalizes ("like for part 2" is now flagged)
5. Do NOT delete and repost the same content -- algorithm tracks this
6. Do NOT switch between business/creator account types frequently
7. Do NOT use VPN while posting -- IP changes trigger flags
8. Do NOT include WhatsApp numbers visually in early posts -- can be flagged as spam/solicitation

| **Algorithmic treatment** | Classified as "advertiser" -- lower organic distribution | Treated as organic creator |

### Reach Suppression Data

- Business account organic reach dropped **30-45%** compared to 2024 levels
- Creator accounts maintained relatively stable performance
- Business accounts appear algorithmically classified as "advertisers"
- The music restriction alone significantly impacts reach -- cannot participate in sound-driven trends

### Recommendation for CatatOrder

**START with a Creator/Personal Account.** Here is why:

1. **4.5x higher engagement rate** (11.29% vs 2.5%) -- critical for a 0-follower account trying to escape 200-view jail
2. **Full music library access** -- can ride trending sounds for discovery
3. **No algorithmic advertiser penalty** -- treated as organic content
4. **You do not need the bio link yet** -- at 0 followers, nobody is clicking your bio
5. **You do not need Ads Manager yet** -- paid ads at low ARPU is anti-pattern (BukuKas burned $140M)

**Switch to Business Account ONLY when:**
- You hit 1,000+ followers (you already have bio link at that point)
- You are ready to run paid ads
- You need advanced analytics for optimization
- Your organic growth strategy is established

**Workaround if you want bio link on creator account:**
- Pin a comment with your link on every video
- Use link-in-bio tool (Linktree, etc.) once you hit 1,000 followers

## 10. TikTok SEO -- How Text on Images Gets Indexed

### How TikTok Reads Your Content

TikTok uses multiple AI systems to index content for search:

1. **OCR (Optical Character Recognition):** Scans ALL text visible in images/video frames
2. **Speech-to-Text:** Transcribes spoken words in audio
3. **Visual Recognition:** Identifies objects, scenes, activities in video
4. **Caption Analysis:** Reads and indexes caption text and hashtags
5. **Audio Fingerprinting:** Identifies music/sounds used

### Where to Place Keywords (Priority Order)

| Location | SEO Weight | Details |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Caption (first 150 chars)** | HIGHEST | Algorithm weights first 150 characters most heavily |
| **On-screen text/overlays** | Very High | OCR reads this -- functions like H1/H2 headings in web SEO |
| **Spoken words in audio** | High | Speech-to-text indexed |
| **Hashtags** | Medium | Now supplementary to keywords |
| **Full caption (150-4000 chars)** | Medium-Low | Still indexed but less weight than first 150 |

### The 400% Effect

**Videos with on-screen text rank 400% higher in TikTok search.** This is the single most impactful SEO tactic.

### Caption Strategy

- TikTok allows **4,000 characters** in captions (up from 2,200)
- **First 150 characters are most important** for search ranking
- Write in natural language, not keyword-stuffed
- Front-load your most important keywords
- Include keywords that match what your audience would SEARCH for

### Keyword Strategy for CatatOrder

**Primary keywords (use in every post):**
- catat pesanan, pencatatan order, order management
- UMKM, usaha kecil, bisnis kuliner, jualan online
- WhatsApp bisnis, kelola pesanan

**Long-tail keywords (rotate by topic):**
- cara catat pesanan otomatis
- aplikasi pesanan WhatsApp
- rekap pesanan harian
- struk digital UMKM
- tips jualan online pemula

### How to Optimize Carousel Images for SEO

1. **Include keywords in text overlays on EVERY slide** -- OCR reads each image separately
2. **Title slide:** Include primary keyword visually (e.g., "Cara Catat Pesanan Otomatis via WhatsApp")
3. **Use clear, large fonts** -- OCR accuracy depends on readability
4. **Repeat key terms** across slides -- reinforces topic signal
5. **Caption:** Write keyword-rich first line, then context, then hashtags at end

### Example Optimized Post Structure

```
CAPTION (first 150 chars):
"Cara catat pesanan otomatis via WhatsApp -- gak perlu tulis manual lagi! UMKM kuliner wajib tau tips ini"

ON-SCREEN TEXT (slide 1):
"5 Cara Catat Pesanan Otomatis"

ON-SCREEN TEXT (slide 2-6):
Each tip with relevant keywords in text overlay

ON-SCREEN TEXT (slide 7):
"Save biar gak lupa! Follow @catatorder"

HASHTAGS (at end of caption):
#CatatOrder #UMKM #catatpesanan #bisniskuliner #jualanonline
```
