# 05 · Pricing

> Pricing yang dignifying, bukan yang memanipulasi.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Apple selalu premium-priced, tapi tidak pernah manipulative. Tidak ada flash sale, tidak ada countdown timer, tidak ada "limited offer." Apple charge fair, deliver excellent, customer pay willingly.

Tokoflow follows: pricing yang **clear, fair, dignifying**.

5 prinsip pricing Tokoflow:

1. **Simple** — 3 tiers maksimum. Bukan 5+ confusing options.
2. **Transparent** — semua harga visible upfront. Tidak ada hidden charge.
3. **Fair value** — pricing match dengan value yang diberikan.
4. **No anxiety** — tidak ada "47/50 used" warning, tidak ada countdown trial.
5. **Dignifying** — user bayar karena worth it, bukan karena dipaksa.

## 3-Tier Structure (Year 1 Malaysia)

| Tier | Price | Untuk |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | RM 0/month | Coba dulu, baru mulai jualan |
| **Pro** | RM 49/month | Active mompreneur, daily driver |
| **Business** | RM 99/month | Growing merchant, multi-staff, compliance |

### Free Tier

**Price**: RM 0/bulan
**Tagline**: "Mulai jualan dengan satu foto. Gratis."

**Includes**:
- ✅ 1-Photo Onboarding
- ✅ 1 toko aktif
- ✅ 50 pesanan/bulan (after that, gentle nudge to upgrade)
- ✅ Basic AI customer assistant (auto-reply standar)
- ✅ Web shop link (`tokoflow.com/your-shop`)
- ✅ DuitNow QR / FPX payment integration
- ✅ Customer database (auto-built from orders)
- ✅ Daily Summary notification
- ⚠️ Watermark "Powered by Tokoflow" di shop page

**NOT included**:
- Voice setup fallback (Pro)
- Photo enhancement (Pro)
- Mobile app native (Pro)—web only at Free
- LHDN MyInvois auto-submit (Business)
- Multi-staff (Business)

**Why Free exists**:
- Wedge tier untuk acquisition
- Magic moment (1-photo onboarding) accessible to all
- Demonstrate value before asking payment
- Apple pakai trial; Tokoflow pakai persistent free dengan limits

---

### Pro Tier

**Price**: RM 49/bulan (~$10 USD)
**Tagline**: "Semua tools, AI lengkap, tanpa watermark."

**Includes everything in Free, plus**:
- ✅ Unlimited pesanan
- ✅ Voice setup fallback (mic input untuk semua interaction)
- ✅ Photo enhancement (AI-enhanced product photos)
- ✅ Mobile app native (iOS + Android)
- ✅ Pricing Whisper (gentle peer benchmarking)
- ✅ Customer Recognition (returning customer features)
- ✅ Smart Reminders (inventory, follow-up)
- ✅ Weekly Story (insight beyond daily summary)
- ✅ No watermark on shop page
- ✅ Email support

**Anchor pricing**:
- 1 jam admin labor di Malaysia ≈ RM 12-15
- RM 49/bulan = harga 4 jam admin
- Tokoflow save 1 jam/hari = 30 jam/bulan
- ROI: ~7x value (clear)

**Marketing line**:
> *"Sehari kamu hemat 1 jam. Sebulan, RM 360 worth of time. Tokoflow Pro RM 49."*

---

### Business Tier

**Price**: RM 99/bulan (~$20 USD)
**Tagline**: "LHDN otomatis. Multi-staff. Pikiran tenang."

**Includes everything in Pro, plus**:
- ✅ LHDN MyInvois auto-submit (compliance silent)
- ✅ SST/PPN auto-calculator
- ✅ Tax reminder & summary
- ✅ Multi-staff accounts (2 staff included, +RM 15/extra)
- ✅ Order assignment to staff
- ✅ Advanced analytics (still dignifying, not data-dump)
- ✅ Priority support (24-hour response)
- ✅ Receipt PDF customization

**Why Business tier exists**:
- Compliance jadi differentiator silent
- Mid-tier merchants RM 1M+ turnover (LHDN urgency)
- Anchor pricing untuk merchants yang hit growth threshold

**Marketing line**:
> *"Saat usahamu tumbuh, kompleksitas tidak harus ikut tumbuh."*

## Anti-Pricing Features

Yang Tokoflow secara sadar **tidak akan pernah** lakukan di pricing:

### Anxiety-Inducing
- ❌ "Trial expires in 3 days" countdown timer
- ❌ "Used 47/50 orders this month" persistent banner
- ❌ "Upgrade now or lose features" warnings
- ❌ Negative trending arrows on usage stats
- ❌ Comparison shaming ("Pro users get 3x more sales!")

### Manipulative
- ❌ Annual billing discount yang nyolok ("Save 50%!")—gentle mention OK, not aggressive
- ❌ Limited time offer pop-ups
- ❌ Dark pattern checkout (pre-checked addons)
- ❌ Autoplay video promo upgrade
- ❌ Fake scarcity ("Only 3 spots left!")

### Confusing
- ❌ More than 3 tiers
- ❌ Per-feature pricing add-ons (Free + RM 5 untuk feature X + RM 8 untuk feature Y)
- ❌ Usage-based billing yang unpredictable
- ❌ Hidden setup fees
- ❌ Cancellation friction (must call support, etc.)

### Disrespectful
- ❌ Auto-renewal tanpa reminder
- ❌ Auto-upgrade trial to paid (require explicit upgrade)
- ❌ Email blast "We miss you, come back!"
- ❌ Account deletion friction

## Pricing Display Principles

### On Pricing Page

```
[Free]              [Pro]               [Business]
RM 0/bulan         RM 49/bulan          RM 99/bulan

(Mulai)            (Pakai Sehari-hari)  (Compliance Lengkap)

✓ ...              ✓ ...                ✓ ...
✓ ...              ✓ ...                ✓ ...
                   ✓ ...                ✓ ...

[Mulai Gratis]     [Coba Pro]          [Coba Business]
```

**Principles**:
- Equal visual weight (no "MOST POPULAR" banner shouting)
- Tier names descriptive ("Pakai Sehari-hari," not "BEST VALUE")
- Feature list complete, no "..." obfuscation
- CTA gentle, not aggressive

### In-App Upgrade Prompt

When user hit Free limit (50 orders/month):

✅ DO:
> *"Ramai ya bulan ini! Sudah 50 pesanan. Kalau mau lanjut tanpa batas, Tokoflow Pro RM 49/bulan."*
> [Upgrade ke Pro] [Lanjutkan dengan limit]

❌ DON'T:
> *"⚠️ QUOTA EXCEEDED! UPGRADE NOW TO CONTINUE!"*

## Refund & Cancellation

### Refund Policy

- 30-day money-back guarantee on Pro and Business tiers
- No questions asked
- Refund processed within 5 business days
- Pro-rated refund jika cancel mid-month

### Cancellation

- Self-service in app (no phone call required)
- 1-tap cancel
- Account remains accessible until period end
- Data export available (downloadable)
- Re-activation easy (within 90 days, all data retained)

**Why generous**: Apple-grade trust. Treat user as adult. They'll come back if product is good.

## Pricing Communication

### Marketing Hierarchy

| Audience | Price emphasis |
|---|---|
| Landing page hero | NO price (tagline only)—"From snap to sold" |
| Landing page mid-section | Mention "Mulai gratis" |
| Pricing page | Full transparent comparison |
| In-app | Contextual, gentle |
| Sales conversation (1-on-1) | Anchored to value (time saved, revenue gained) |

**NOT**:
- ❌ Price as primary marketing message ("Cheapest in Malaysia!")
- ❌ Discount-led campaigns
- ❌ "Save XXX%" banners

### Honest Comparison vs Competitors

If asked "compared to Linktree/Mayar/Wati":

✅ DO: 
> "Linktree shows your links, Tokoflow handles the whole sale. Different category, different value. RM 49 vs $9 (Linktree Pro) reflects what's included."

❌ DON'T:
> "Cheaper than Wati ($99)!"—race to bottom, undignifying.

## Pricing Update Protocol

| Change | Process |
|---|---|
| Adjust pricing within ±10% | Aldi decision, log in [`07-decisions.md`](./07-decisions.md) |
| Adjust pricing >10% | Diskusi formal, market validation, give existing users grandfathered rate for 12 months |
| Add new tier | Strategic decision—may affect positioning, requires positioning review |
| Remove tier | Major decision—communicate to users 90 days advance |
| Geographic pricing (new country) | Per-country research, cost-of-living adjusted |
