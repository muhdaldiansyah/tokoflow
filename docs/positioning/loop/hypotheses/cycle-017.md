## 1 refinement queued for cycle 20

### First-launch sensory ritual

The first time a merchant opens the app, show a **3-second sensory demonstration with no purpose** — just to feel the haptic + sound + visual signature once. Like Apple's first-boot setup that shows you the gestures.

Specifically: on first launch, animation plays the 3-sensory filing reveal exactly once with a placeholder card (e.g., "Sample order · RM 1 · paid"), then prompts: *"You'll feel this every time you tell me something. Tap anywhere to begin."*

Why: sets sensory expectation BEFORE first real capture. By the time merchant records her first real voice note, she already knows what the reveal feels like. The expectation amplifies the magic.

This is a 1-line addition to Day 1 onboarding, not a positioning change.
