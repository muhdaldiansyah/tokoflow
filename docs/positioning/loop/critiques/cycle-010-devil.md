# Cycle 010 — YC Partner Round 3 Final Review

**Reviewer stance:** sat through 2 rounds. Round 2 closed at MAYBE: "moat coherent, math says lifestyle." This is the deciding round.

## Q1 — vs Bukku-with-voice (sev-8 round 2 challenge)

**Honest, mostly.** The table isn't hand-wave because each row maps to a real revenue dependency competitors can't shed in 90 days:

- Accountant primary-user → Bukku's renewal cohort. Pivoting home screen to "voice notes feed" abandons the SME accountant SKU that pays today.
- UOB co-marketing → contractually requires P&L surface, not diary. Banks don't promote "tukang dengar."
- Lock-in retention → 1-tap export is structurally incompatible with their NRR model.

What it *doesn't* address: Bukku could ship a **second SKU** ("Bukku Lite for Mompreneur") in 6 months and burn $500K to buy TikTok creators. The 90-day claim holds; the 12-month claim is shakier. **Sev 6, not killer.**

## Q2 — Bank-channel asymmetry

**Plausible defense, not capitulation.** "Channel-orthogonal" is the right framing because UOB SmartBusiness audience (registered SMEs with bank accounts, BRN, SST-curious) is genuinely a different human than the Shah Alam mompreneur on TikTok who hasn't filed Sdn Bhd. Different acquisition surface, different willingness-to-pay, different onboarding shape.

Caveat: doc admits 4 named creators, **none contacted**. Until one creator posts and conversion data exists, "channel-orthogonal" is hypothesis, not defense. **Sev 7.** This is the single biggest unproven claim in the deck.

## Q3 — Completeness moat (iPod lesson)

**Real moat, conditionally.** The iPod analogy works if Day 1 actually ships finished — voice + diary-IS-DB + WA Share + lock-screen + photo + briefing + refuse-list trust + signature animation. That's a coherent product, not a feature list. A funded competitor adding "voice" to an existing accounting product gets voice-as-feature, which is exactly the asymmetry the doc names.

Risk: 2 engineers × 3 weeks for that scope is **aggressive to the point of fiction.** Lock-screen widget alone (iOS Live Activities + Android quick settings tile + biometric + offline queue) is 3 weeks. Realistic: 2 eng × 10–12 weeks. Doesn't kill the moat, but kills the schedule. **Sev 5.**

## Q4 — Cycle 9 cost-stack hardening (on-device STT)

**Materially changes the math.** Voice → on-device Whisper → text-only LLM payload is ~70% AI cost reduction vs audio-upload. At RM 25 → RM 7–8/merchant/month, Free tier becomes structurally sustainable, and Pro RM 49 carries 80%+ gross margin even at heavy usage.

This is the round-2 "lifestyle business" Series A objection partially answered: gross-margin headroom now exists. Doesn't fix CAC or TAM, but removes the unit-economics floor concern. **Round 2 sev-8 → sev 4.**
