# 01 · Positioning

> Siapa Tokoflow untuk siapa, kategori apa, beda dari apa.

## Positioning Statement (Strategic, internal)

> **Tokoflow menangani mechanical residue (admin, payment, invoice, pajak, status) untuk solo makers—invisibly, di belakang. Customer relationship tetap milikmu—kami suggest, kamu yang send. Pure craft (yang kamu cintai) dilindungi: Tokoflow tidak masuk ke dapurmu. Satu foto untuk mulai. AI handle admin, kamu kembali ke meja kerja.**

4 elemen kunci dari statement ini:

1. **"Mechanical residue invisibly"** → yang Tokoflow hilangkan secara autonomous. Tier 3 dari [Three-Tier Reality](./00-manifesto.md#the-three-tier-reality).
2. **"Customer relationship tetap milikmu"** → trust dengan customer **tidak diserahkan ke AI**. Kami amplifi (suggest), tidak menggantikan (send). Tier 2.
3. **"Pure craft dilindungi"** → Tokoflow tidak masuk ke kitchen, studio, workshop. Tier 1 sacred.
4. **"Satu foto untuk mulai"** → magic moment. The Photo Magic.

Pemahaman ini menggantikan framing lama "AI handle everything for you" yang melanggar trust transfer pada relationship layer.

## Tagline (Customer-facing)

### Primary brand tagline
> **"We handle the receipts. Not the recipes."**

Bahasa: ***"Resi kami urus. Resep kamu."***

Kenapa ini primary brand:
- **Specific, not generic** — bisa dibedakan dari setiap competitor SaaS lain
- **Kitchen line literal di dalam tagline** — recipes = craft (sacred), receipts = mechanical residue (we handle)
- **Memorable** — alliteration (receipts/recipes) makes it stick
- **Implies refuse list** — we handle ONE thing, not everything; restraint as feature
- **Translates** — Bahasa version preserves the metaphor (Resi/Resep parallel)
- **Universal evolution** — for non-F&B (Wave 4 creator/freelancer): "We handle the paperwork. Not the work." atau adaptasi serupa

### Feature-level tagline (untuk Photo Magic spesifik)
> **"From snap to sold."**

Tetap dipakai untuk Photo Magic onboarding interaction. Itu describing magic moment, bukan brand promise.

### Alternate (untuk konteks beda)
- **"The paperwork, not the kitchen."** (English-tighter alternative)
- **"Your kitchen. Our paperwork."** (possessive, even crisper)
- **"Your shop. One photo away."** (untuk Photo Magic hero)
- **"Sell anything. Just snap."** (CTA Photo Magic)

### Tagline yang TIDAK dipakai (anti)
- ❌ "The best WhatsApp commerce tool" — categorizes us as WA wrapper
- ❌ "LHDN-ready POS for Malaysia" — too technical, too narrow
- ❌ "Manage your orders effortlessly" — generic, fungsional
- ❌ "All-in-one selling platform" — tidak Apple-grade restraint
- ❌ "AI runs your business" — over-claim, melanggar trust transfer
- ❌ "Get back to making" — too aspirational, less concrete
- ❌ "Less admin. More making." — generic; any productivity tool bisa pakai (per critique 2026-04-28)

## Target User (Year 1 — laser focus)

### Primary Persona: Bu Aisyah

> Wanita, 32 tahun, ibu 2 anak, tinggal di Shah Alam Selangor. Dulu accountant, sekarang full-time ibu rumah tangga + jualan kek lapis & kuih ros dari rumah. Order ramai dari WA group ibu-ibu sekolah anak + kawan kantor lama. Bulan biasa Rp 8-15jt revenue. Bulan Ramadan/Raya bisa Rp 30-50jt. Pakai HP Android. Aktif IG (1,200 followers) + WA Status. Tidak pakai Instagram Reels. Belum pernah denger Linktree. Tidak punya KTP/SST registered.

**Profil teknis:**
- Device: Android mid-tier (Samsung A series, Xiaomi)
- Apps daily: WhatsApp, Instagram, kamera HP, banking app
- Digital sophistication: medium (bisa edit foto, tidak bisa coding)
- Pain paling akut: chat WA bertumpuk saat ramai, lupa pesanan, hitung manual capek

**Profil emosi:**
- Bangga sebagai pengusaha rumah, tapi kadang merasa "cuma kecil-kecilan"
- Butuh tools yang **memuliakan** identitas pengusaha-nya, bukan yang mengkonfirmasi "kamu small business"
- Tidak punya banyak waktu/energi belajar tools baru yang ribet

**Three-Tier Reality untuk Bu Aisyah** (observasi):
- **Tier 1 (Pure Craft)**: ~4 jam/hari di dapur, baking. Yang dia cintai, alasan dia mulai.
- **Tier 2 (Customer Relationship)**: ~2 jam/hari ngobrol dengan customer. Banyak dari ini DIA HARGAI—Pak Andi loyal, school moms WA group, kawan kantor lama. Bukan beban, bagian identity-nya.
- **Tier 3 (Mechanical Residue)**: ~3-4 jam/hari payment matching, copy invoice, hitung pajak, update status, jawab "berapa harga" untuk ke-50 kali, recall pelanggan setia. **Yang dia bukan cinta, bukan hargai—yang dia harus selesaikan.**

**Tokoflow target Tier 3 untuk hilang. Tier 2 dilindungi. Tier 1 dikembalikan.**

> ⚠️ **PERSONA UNVALIDATED.** Sampai 2026-04-28, persona ini disusun dari observasi sekunder + theory. Phase 0 wajib validate dengan 10 deep interviews (5 friendly + 5 hostile) sebelum commit ke build. Lihat [`06-roadmap.md`](./06-roadmap.md) Phase 0.

**Pricing power:**
- Rp 50K/bulan terasa investasi, Rp 200K/bulan harus pikir keras
- Mau bayar lebih kalau saving time-nya jelas (1 jam/hari = Rp 100K worth)
- Tidak rela bayar kalau cuma "nice to have"

### Secondary Persona (Year 2+)

- **Online fashion/kosmetik reseller** (similar profile, lebih digital savvy)
- **Modest fashion seller** (Muslimah segment, community-strong)
- **Catering bulanan** (B2B-light, monthly contract pattern)

### NOT target Year 1
- ❌ Warung kopi / kopitiam (POS-heavy, different need)
- ❌ Mamak / hawker (cash-dominant)
- ❌ B2B wholesale (different scale, different needs)
- ❌ Korporat / multi-outlet (Shopify territory)
- ❌ Influencer / creator (Linktree territory)

**Disiplin Year 1: laser focus pada Bu Aisyah dan dia saja.** Setiap fitur diuji: apakah Bu Aisyah butuh ini?

## Real Moat (sharp articulation, refined 2026-04-28)

> Critique yang valid: BukuKas, Majoo, Moka, Storehub semua bisa payment matching, invoicing, tax, stock. "AI does the work" adalah commodity claim. Real moat must be specific.

Real moat Tokoflow ada di 3 dimensi yang **bersamaan**:

| Moat dimension | What competitors do | What Tokoflow does |
|---|---|---|
| **Input format** | Structured forms, manual entry | **Unstructured input parsing** — WA chat screenshots, voice notes, mixed e-wallet receipts (DuitNow + GrabPay + cash). AI handles the messy real-world input. |
| **Conversational UX** | English/formal BM forms | **Bahasa-first, Manglish-tolerant** — code-switch BM/EN/Mandarin natural. Built for how mompreneur actually talks. |
| **Compliance integration** | Niagawan: form-heavy hero | **Silent, gated to Pro+** — only surfaces when merchant approaches threshold. Free tier merchants never see TIN/BRN/SST forms. |
| **Buyer experience** | Configure storefront manually | **Storefront generated from photo, conversational order flow** for customer (Photo Magic + AI Hybrid Order Flow) |

**The unifying claim**: AI doesn't change the existence of admin tools. AI changes **what kind of input becomes acceptable**. Where competitors require humans to clean & structure data first, Tokoflow takes the messy reality (chats, voice, photos) and handles it.

**Naval extension** ([from 00-manifesto](./00-manifesto.md#mission-statement)): AI labor as 5th leverage rung is the *underlying enabling shift*. The 4-dimensional moat is *what we build on top of it*.

## Kategori Tokoflow

Bukan link aggregator. Bukan POS. Bukan e-commerce platform. Bukan CRM.

**Kategori baru: "Complete Selling Layer for Individuals."**

Posisi di market spectrum:

```
Link aggregators (Linktree, Bento, Beacons)
         ↓ (limited shop features)
Light commerce (Linktree Shop, Stan Store, Mayar)
         ↓ (more selling, less integrated)
Storefront builders (Saleor, payhip, Shopify Lite)
         ↓ (configuration heavy)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
       ⬇ Tokoflow ⬇  ← kategori baru
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
         ↑ (overkill for individuals)
Full e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)
```

Tokoflow lebih lengkap dari Linktree, lebih simple dari Shopify, **AI-native** (yang keduanya tidak).

## Anti-Positioning (yang Tokoflow BUKAN)

Kategori yang sengaja kita reject:

### ❌ Bukan Linktree alternative
**Why salah**: Linktree = aggregator link. Tokoflow = complete selling experience. Membandingkan apple to orange. Lebih lanjut: [Linktree comparison](#linktree-comparison-detail).

### ❌ Bukan Shopify untuk SEA
**Why salah**: Shopify untuk merchant yang siap configure (themes, plugins, apps). Tokoflow untuk individu yang **tidak mau configure apa-apa**. Beda philosophy.

### ❌ Bukan WhatsApp Business tools
**Why salah**: Wati, Qontak, SleekFlow target merchants yang bangun chatbot WA. Tokoflow tidak require WA. Channel-agnostic.

### ❌ Bukan POS / kasir digital
**Why salah**: Mokapos, Pawoon target physical store dengan hardware. Tokoflow phone-first, no hardware.

### ❌ Bukan CRM untuk SMB
**Why salah**: Salesforce/Hubspot untuk B2B sales pipeline. Tokoflow untuk B2C order operations.

### ❌ Bukan freemium-with-anxiety SaaS
**Why salah**: "Used 47/50 orders" = anxiety. Tokoflow = generous Free tier dengan jelas, upgrade hanya saat genuinely needed.

## Linktree Comparison (Detail)

Customer & merchant akan compare ke Linktree (familiar reference). Berikut framing-nya:

### Permukaan: similar
- Both share-able link from IG/TikTok bio
- Both have "shop page" concept
- Both work without customer install

### Fundamental: different

| Aspek | Linktree | Tokoflow |
|---|---|---|
| Setup | Drag-drop links manual | 1-photo AI auto-generate |
| Customer journey | Klik → list link → external | Klik → order langsung di sini |
| Communication | Tidak ada (redirect WA) | AI handle chat customer |
| Payment | Redirect to external | Native instant QRIS/FPX |
| Order management | Tidak ada | Full backend |
| Compliance | Tidak ada | LHDN auto (Business tier) |
| Mobile app for owner | Tidak ada | Native immersive |
| AI | Tidak ada | Core (companion + voice) |
| Empathy layer | Stats dashboard | Daily summary, dignifying |
| Pricing | $5-24/mo (US-pricing) | RM 49-99/mo |

### Marketing positioning vs Linktree

**Strategi A — Lean into comparison** (untuk press, demo, social):
> "Linktree showed the world your links. Tokoflow shows the world your shop. And handles everything else."

**Strategi B — Avoid comparison** (untuk landing page hero):
> "Sell anything. From snap to sold."
> Biar customer judge sendiri tanpa anchor ke kompetitor (Apple way).

**Default**: Strategi B di marketing publik. Strategi A boleh untuk konteks edukasi (1-on-1 demo, podcast interview, dll).
