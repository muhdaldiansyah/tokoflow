## 1. GTM strategy overview

Berdasarkan analisis dari [07-positioning.md](./07-positioning.md) (Opsi B: home F&B wedge) dan [06-harga-ekonomi-unit.md](./06-harga-ekonomi-unit.md) (organic-first mandatory):

**GTM principles:**

1. **Organic-dominant 0-9 months** — paid ads hanya setelah organic flywheel proven
2. **BM-first, hyper-localized** content — tidak cukup translate ID
3. **Community-led** — Ariff's network + WA group seeding
4. **Partner leverage** — MDEC Digitalisation Partner + bank/association referral
5. **Narrow segment first** — home F&B Selangor/KL/Johor, bukan broad MY

## 2. Target segment profile (ICP)

**Primary ICP: "Kak Long Kuih" persona**

- **Demographics:** Perempuan 28-45 tahun, tinggal di Selangor/KL/Johor/Penang
- **Bisnis:** Home-based kuih/kek/frozen food/dropship reseller, <RM 300K revenue
- **Tech behavior:** Pakai WA Business (free app), Instagram untuk jualan, FB groups untuk komuniti
- **Pain points:**
  - "Susah nak ingat siapa pesan apa"
  - "Nak kira jual-beli semua guna kalkulator"
  - "Pelanggan selalu tanya 'berapa harga?' untuk item yang sama"
- **Current tools:** WhatsApp Business, Excel, Notes app, paper notebook
- **Language:** BM primary, English secondary

**Secondary ICP: "Abang Dropship" persona**

- **Demographics:** Lelaki 25-40, peninsular MY
- **Bisnis:** Dropship fashion/gadget/beauty, order via WA/IG, <RM 500K revenue
- **Tech behavior:** TikTok-heavy content creator, joined reseller WA groups
- **Pain points:**
  - "Nak track siapa bayar, siapa belum"
  - "Banyak group order, pening nak atur"
  - "Susah kira profit tiap bulan"

**Tertiary ICP: "Warung tradisional pindah digital"**

- Coffee shop, nasi campur, small kedai runcit trying to go digital
- Usually discovered via local community or bank program

## 3. Primary channels (ranked by priority)

### Channel 1 — Community seeding via Ariff's network (Phase 1 recon)

**Tactic:** Ariff recruit 15 home-biz tester dari kampus + personal network. Each tester:
- Onboard CatatOrder free (BM-first version)
- Usage 4 weeks minimum
- 30-min interview + written feedback
- Optional video testimonial BM (RM 50 incentive)

**Target:** 15 users, 10 active, 5 testimonial.
**CAC:** RM 0-500 total budget (primarily incentive).
**Expected paid conversion:** 2-3 dari 15 become paying post-launch.

### Channel 2 — TikTok Bahasa Melayu organic content

**Tactic:**
- 3 post/week (Reel format, 30-60 sec)
- Content pillars:
  - "Home-biz owner review" — user testimonial
  - "Tutorial terima order di WA dengan CatatOrder"
  - "Tip kira untung untuk biz rumah"
  - "Before/after: catatan tangan vs CatatOrder"
- Hashtags: #usahawanbm #homebiz #biznesrumah #ibuusahawan #kuih #frozenfood
- Hook BM: "Eh, jangan lagi catat pesanan dalam kertas la..."

**Expected:** 10K views/video at baseline, 100K viral breakout monthly.
**CAC via TikTok organic:** RM 100-250/paying customer.
**Team cost:** 1 content creator part-time RM 1,500-2,500/month.

### Channel 3 — Facebook group seeding

**Primary groups to seed:**
- Entrepreneurs and Startups Malaysia
- Malaysia SMEs & Micro SMEs
- Malaysia Business Group
- MAFEA Malaysia (Malay Future Entrepreneur Assoc.)
- Usahawan Kuih Malaysia (niche)
- Reseller Dropship Bukan Puteri (niche)
- Ibu Bapa Bisnes Rumahan (niche)

**Tactic:** 
- Ariff atau community manager engage 1-2 post/day genuine (question, share experience)
- Once-per-week soft mention CatatOrder in relevant thread
- Monthly "case study" post with user win

**Expected CAC:** RM 50-150 (mostly time, not cash).
**Risk:** Group admin ban if too promotional. Must be genuine community contribution first.

### Channel 4 — Instagram Reel BM + IG partner creator

**Tactic:**
- Partner with 3-5 micro-influencer BM (5K-30K followers, home-biz niche)
- Product seed (free Bisnis tier + RM 200 cash honorarium)
- Content deliverable: 1 Reel review + 3 Story
- Target creators: "ibu usahawan kuih", "home baker reviewer", "reseller influencer"

**Expected:** 3-5 partnership × 10K reach each = 30-50K impressions.
**CAC per converted:** RM 200-400.
**Budget:** RM 1,500-2,500 per cohort.

### Channel 5 — PMKS Digital Grant MADANI referral

**Tactic:**
- Register CatatOrder sebagai MDEC Digitalisation Partner (Phase 1 action)
- Customer-facing messaging: "Dapat diskon 50% melalui subsidi PMKS MADANI"
- Onboarding flow untuk subsidy-eligible users (SSM-registered + RM 50K turnover)

**Expected:** 20-30% of paid signups via subsidy messaging.
**CAC:** RM 50-150 (mostly paperwork time).
**Strategic importance:** **Unique advantage** — competitors rarely promote this.

### Channel 6 — Referral program

**Mechanics:**
- Existing user refer new user via unique link
- Both sides: RM 30 credit (toward Isi Ulang atau subscription)
- No cap on referrals
- Bonus: 3+ referrals = free Growth month
- 5+ referrals = free Bisnis month + "Champion" badge (community status)

**Expected:** 25-40% of new signups from referral at steady state (Month 9+).
**CAC:** RM 30 direct cost + ~RM 20 fulfillment = RM 50/paying customer.

### Channel 7 — Partner play (Phase 3)

Long-term moves to match Bukku's bank partnership scale:

**Tier 1 partners (aim for 1 by month 18):**
- **Bank Islam** / **Bank Rakyat** — Malay-Muslim SME heavy customer base
- **BSN** — already distributes PMKS MADANI grants
- **Touch 'n Go Business** — merchant network

**Tier 2 partners (opportunistic):**
- **Funding Societies** — already Bukku partner, but may bundle CatatOrder for their borrowers
- **Maxis / CelcomDigi Business** — telco SME programs
- **SME Corp** — thought leadership content partner

**Tier 3 (community):**
- **MAFEA, SME Assoc MY** — speaker slots + sponsored event
- **Persatuan Usahawan Bumiputera** — direct Malay-Bumiputera reach

## 4. Messaging playbook (BM)

### Headline (BM)

**"Catat pesanan WhatsApp, bukukan sendiri. Gratis sampai 50 pesanan sebulan."**

Atau opsi alternatif:

- "Bisnes rumah makin teratur — pesanan, pelanggan, duit semua tersimpan"
- "Dari catat kertas ke CatatOrder. Simpler la"

### Value props ranked

1. **"Gratis"** — 34% think cloud expensive, this matters
2. **"Khas untuk bisnes rumah"** — segmentation signal
3. **"Tiada perlu akaun"** — frictionless onboarding (vs Bukku accountant feel)
4. **"Terima pesanan dari WhatsApp langsung"** — obvious fit
5. **"Ada panduan dalam Bahasa Melayu"** — BM-first USP

### Messaging DON'Ts

- ❌ "Accounting software" — triggers Bukku comparison, we lose
- ❌ "E-invoice compliant" — irrelevant + we're not compliant anyway
- ❌ "POS replacement" — wrong segment
- ❌ "Best untuk SME" — too broad, we target micro
- ❌ English-first copy — 60% of target miss it

### Tone

- Warm, conversational ("kamu" or "awak" — test both in BM)
- Never corporate/B2B stiff
- Visual: ibu rumah tangga, kuih, dapur, tangan hold phone — bukan office setting

## 5. Sales / conversion funnel

### Top-of-funnel

- **Sources:** TikTok views, FB group clicks, IG Reel, referral link
- **Landing:** `catatorder.my` with BM copy + 1 CTA "Mula Percuma"
- **Target conversion to signup:** 12%+ (healthy B2B SaaS)

### Mid-funnel: Free tier activation

- Sign up → 2-minute onboarding → create first order form → share via WA
- **Target:** 60%+ activation (at least 1 real order within 7 days)
- Push notification nudge: "Eh, dah 3 hari. Nak cuba hantar order pertama?"

### Conversion to paid

- Triggers to upgrade:
  - Hit 50 orders (free limit) → Isi Ulang RM 9 natural upgrade
  - Month 2 retention → Starter RM 19 monthly
  - Add a customer to list → Growth RM 39 (CRM feature gated)
- **Target Free → Paid conversion:** 15-20% at month 3

### Retention + upsell

- Weekly "rekap" push — "Minggu ni kamu terima X pesanan"
- Monthly report auto-generated
- Starter → Growth upsell: "Nak lagi banyak fitur? Upgrade ke Growth"
- Community tier: Invite existing user to organize local group

- [ ] TikTok account + 10 videos prepared (drip-release)

### T-7 days

- [ ] Soft launch: Ariff's 15 testers onboard first
- [ ] 5 case study videos published (BM, with testimonials)
- [ ] Community manager hired part-time (BM-native)
- [ ] Instagram page live with 15 seed posts
- [ ] 3 FB group admins contacted for announcement post

### Day 0 — Public launch

- [ ] TikTok live launch video (BM, 60 sec)
- [ ] Facebook post di 5 primary SME groups
- [ ] Instagram Reel × 5 micro-influencers go live
- [ ] Email blast ke 15 testers: "Kamu antara yang pertama pakai"

### Week 1-4

- [ ] Monitor signups, activation, retention daily
- [ ] Respond to every comment/DM within 2 hours

### Month 2-3

- [ ] Subsidy integration live (MDEC DP status must be ready)
- [ ] Referral program launch

## 10. What to avoid (common MY SaaS GTM mistakes)

- ❌ **English-first marketing** — misses 60% target audience
- ❌ **Corporate B2B tone** — wrong register for home-biz
- ❌ **Lead magnet PDF download** — SME doesn't read PDFs, they watch TikTok
- ❌ **Webinar strategy** — too formal, use Instagram Live instead
- ❌ **Paid Facebook ads Week 1** — organic first, paid once you know what converts
- ❌ **Press release launch** — fly under radar, don't wake up Orderla.my
- ❌ **Feature comparison chart** — focus on outcome ("teratur pesanan") not specs
- ❌ **Generic Southeast Asia positioning** — be explicitly MY, BM-first

## 11. Post-launch competitive moves

**Month 6 checkpoint:**
- If 500+ paying users: publish "How CatatOrder compares to Orderla.my" content
- If <200 paying users: reassess wedge, consider pivot to sub-segment

**Month 12:**
- If scale: partner pitch deck ready (Bank Islam, MAFEA, SME Assoc)
- If traction flat: sunset mode, maintain existing users, reallocate to ID
