## Distribution thesis

**Channel mix** (TikTok primary; IG/FB cross-post; WhatsApp komuniti penetration):

1. **TikTok founder-led** — Aldi visible founder. 60s vlogs, raw not polished. Topics: founder origin, Three-Tier framework via Bu Aisyah hypothetical, paste-WA-chat demo, restraint list 10 items, behind-scenes manual twin (sanitized), week vlog, viral-attempt hooks.
2. **WhatsApp komuniti penetration** — Aldi joins komuniti masjid Shah Alam + FB Mommies Daily MY + Ibu-Ibu Bisnes Online MY + regional KL Selangor groups. Member-first, helpful posts, NOT promotional. Trust transfer 4-8 weeks.
3. **IG cross-post** — same content, IG Reels format. Bonus reach.
4. **TikTok creator partnership (deferred Week 5+)** — 30-50 outreach to micro-creators (5K-15K followers) for 5 partnerships. Realistic 10-20% conversion. Budget RM 500-2000/creator/month if cash-required.

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Run paid ads to inflate follower count (defeats purpose of validation)
- ❌ Polish content to "professional" — raw founder voice is the asset
- ❌ Sales pitch in posts — Substack restraint, not Owner combat
- ❌ Promote Tokoflow product features — promote Three-Tier framework + mompreneur empathy
- ❌ Indonesian leakage — BM/Manglish/English only, no Bahasa Indonesia register
- ❌ Buy followers, engagement pods, or any growth hack that damages signal integrity

**Allowed**: ONE surgical paid test Week 5 or 6, max RM 500, with explicit learning objective (e.g., "test which of 3 hooks resonates with Klang Valley mompreneur"). Document objective + learning before spending.
