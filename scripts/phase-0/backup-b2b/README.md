# Phase 0.B — Backup Direct B2B Outreach Playbook

> **Activation only**. This playbook activates if and only if:
>
> - Aldi confirms distribution skill **scenario (c)** pre-Phase 0 (no content track record + not committing 8 weeks skill build), OR
> - Distribution kill trigger #6 fires Week 5 (0 inbound DM by end of Week 5), OR
> - Phase 0 Week 8 gate fails (<300 followers AND <15 inbound DM cumulative)
>
> **Strategic context**: [SYNTHESIS-2026-05-05.md §7.5](../../../docs/SYNTHESIS-2026-05-05.md). Pre-drafted as 30-min insurance against 2 weeks confusion if triggered.

---

## Mode

Aldi visits home F&B merchants in Shah Alam in person, pitches Tokoflow, recruits alpha testers. Replaces Track 0.9 (TikTok/komuniti) without changing Tracks 0.1-0.6, 0.7-0.8.

**Distribution thesis (alternative)**: peer-to-peer trust transfer in tight komuniti masjid network beats content-led acquisition at this ARPU + persona. Smaller ceiling but higher conversion.

---

## Geographic plan

4 Shah Alam neighborhoods chosen for home F&B mompreneur density:

1. **Section 7** — established residential, masjid komuniti
2. **Section 13** — middle-income, kueh/kuih cluster
3. **U10** (Subang Bestari area) — younger demographic, IG-active
4. **Bukit Bintang / Kelana Jaya nearby** — overflow if needed

12-13 merchants visited per neighborhood = **50 merchants total**.

Pace: 2-3 visits/day, 10-15 days total spread over Week 4-7 (if mid-Phase-0 activation) or condensed if Week 8 gate fail.

---

## Outreach format

**Primary**: warm introduction via Aldi/Ariff komuniti masjid network. SEA tight komuniti = peer reference works; cold call usually unnecessary.

**Secondary**: Foodpanda/GrabFood listing reconnaissance — identify home F&B merchants by listed address, drop in physically.

**Tertiary**: door-to-door walk in obvious home F&B neighborhood (signs, IG sticker on door).

---

## Qualification criteria (must hit 3 of 5 to qualify)

1. **Pre-order/catering model** (not walk-in shop) — workflow predictability matches Tier-3 thesis
2. **30-150 orders/month volume** — sweet spot where admin pain is felt
3. **WhatsApp + IG primary channels** — not heavy Foodpanda dependent
4. **Solo operator** (not multi-staff restaurant)
5. **Pain language**: spontaneously mentions admin overload / repeat customer chasing / payment confusion

**Disqualify if**:
- Multi-outlet operation (Owner.com territory, not Tokoflow)
- Generic e-commerce reseller (Mekari/EasyStore territory)
- <30 orders/month (too early; refer to free tier no-pitch)
- Pure dine-in (different jobs-to-be-done)

## 15-min demo flow

| Min | Action |
|---|---|
| 1-2 | Introduce self. Ask about her workflow today. Listen. |
| 3-5 | Listen to 1-2 specific Tier-3 admin pain points. Don't pitch yet. |
| 6-10 | Show paste WA chat → order parsing demo on phone. Use her real chat if she's willing. |
| 11-13 | Show Customer DB + repeat order use case. Tie back to her pain. |
| 14-15 | Ask: "would you be willing to be alpha tester? Free Pro tier 3 months." Get yes/no/maybe. |

**Do NOT**:
- Pitch features in min 1-5 (listen first)
- Talk about Three-Tier framework explicitly (too abstract for 15-min)
- Mention LHDN MyInvois unless she brings up tax (silent superpower stays silent)
- Discuss pricing beyond "free 3 months Pro then RM 49/month"

**Bring**:
- Phone with Tokoflow demo loaded (offline-capable if dev not ready)
- Printed 1-pager: BM tagline + 10-item refusal list + Aldi WhatsApp contact
- Notion/Google Sheets tracking spreadsheet open on phone for post-visit log

## Conversion path

Alpha tester commits:
- 3-month Pro free trial starting [date]
- Bring 1 friend mompreneur to next demo (referral seed) — viral coefficient amplifier
- Aldi WhatsApp follow-up within 48 hours
- Weekly check-in for 4 weeks
- Optional case study after 3 months

After 3 months:
- Auto-roll to Pro RM 49 (with merchant consent given upfront)
- OR cancel cleanly, no friction
- OR refer-and-extend (3 more months free for each merchant referred who converts)

## Cost / time

- **Time**: ~25 hours total visiting (50 visits × 30 min average including travel)
- **Travel**: RM 200-400 fuel/Grab Klang Valley
- **Print**: RM 50 1-pager × 100 copies
- **No paid ads**

Total: under RM 500. Within Phase 0 budget cap.

---

## Backup kill criterion

50 visits done, **<5 alpha tester commitments** = product-market fit weak even in best-fit segment via best-fit channel.

→ Kill Phase 1 build.
→ Return to wedge re-spec (sub-niche may be wrong — try frozen food only? Catering only? Wedding cake only?).
→ Or kill product entirely and document learnings to D-019.

No rationalization. No "but they didn't understand the demo." 5 commits from 50 best-fit visits is a low bar; failing it is signal.

|  | Track 0.9 (content-led) | Track 0.B (B2B backup) |
|---|---|---|
| Skill required | Content creation, audience building | B2B sales, in-person rapport |
| Time horizon | 8 weeks compounding | 4 weeks intense |
| Cost | RM 500 max optional ads | RM 500 transport + print |

Choose based on actual founder skill state, not aspiration.
