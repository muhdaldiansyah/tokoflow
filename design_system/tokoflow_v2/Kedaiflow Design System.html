Tokoflow Design System · Foundations

The building blocks of every Tokoflow surface.

Tokoflow helps independent Malaysian businesses take orders, manage customers, and grow on their own terms. This system codifies the visual and interaction language used across the website, order admin, mobile order page, invoices, and WhatsApp templates — so every touchpoint feels like one product.

01 · Identity

Logo & identity

The Tokoflow mark is a stylised ‘K’ built from three strokes — vertical stem, ascending bar, descending leg — implying upward movement and a steady base. Use the wordmark wherever space allows; fall back to the mark only when you need a square or sub-32px footprint.

09 · Motion

Motion

Motion in Tokoflow is functional, not decorative. The default easing is --ease-out — fast acceleration, soft landing — which mirrors how interactions feel: respond immediately, settle gracefully.

Durations how long

TokenValueUse for
--dur-instant60 msHover color swaps, tap pulses (no perceived delay)
--dur-fast120 msButton states, tag toggles, focus rings
--dur-base200 msDefault — tabs, accordions, panel switches
--dur-slow320 msSheets, drawers, page transitions
--dur-slower480 msModal in, confetti / payment-confirmed moments

Easings how it moves

--ease-outDefault · 0.22, 1, 0.36, 1
--ease-in-outLoops · 0.65, 0, 0.35, 1
--ease-springPlayful confirms · 0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1

Patterns recipes

PatternRecipeWhen

Reduced motion: always honour prefers-reduced-motion. Replace transforms with opacity-only fades; never disable the resulting state change.

Hit targets accessibility

02 · Voice

Tone of voice

Three attributes balanced together — the easiest test is the “reading it back to a friend” test. If the sentence makes you sound colder, more boastful, or more uncertain than you would in person, rewrite it.

Supportive

We’re here to help Malaysian businesses grow with confidence. Never lecture, never patronise.

Sounds like
“Looks like the payment didn’t go through. No worries — let’s try again.”
Attribute 01Warm but not saccharine

Efficient

Streamlined tools and workflows that save time and reduce friction. Short sentences. Active voice.

Sounds like
“Payment received. Order moved to Confirmed.”
Attribute 02Cut every word that isn’t needed

Trustworthy

Reliable, transparent, secure. When something goes wrong, we say so clearly — and what we’ll do next.

Sounds like
“DuitNow is slow right now. We’ll auto-confirm as soon as it clears.”
Attribute 03Honest about what we can’t do

Voice in practice do / don’t

Do
“Payment received — RM 73.00 from Aldi.”
“Couldn’t reach the bank. Try again in a moment?”
“Share your order link to start taking orders.”
Plain, concrete, conversational. Lead with what happened.
Don’t
“Successful transaction completed for the amount of RM73.”
“Error: payment provider unreachable. Please retry your operation.”
“Empower your business with our state-of-the-art platform.”
Robotic, jargon-heavy, marketing-speak. None of this is how you’d talk to a friend.

05 · Imagery

Photography direction

Real Malaysian sellers, real products, real moments. We don’t stage abstract office-stock or glossy product cutouts — our customers don’t live there. Warm light, natural backgrounds, hands in frame.

Subject in action
Mood · Warm, golden hourPeople doing the work
Product in context
Mood · Natural backdropKitchen, market, workshop
Interface in hand
Mood · Quiet, calmPhone-first compositions
Do

✓ Show specific Malaysian sellers, named, with permission

✓ Frame the moment: a phone receiving an order, hands packing a box

✓ Keep warm, natural lighting and real settings

✓ Use tight crops on product details when no story is needed

Don’t

✗ Stock photos of generic Westerners in glass offices

✗ Cut-out products on pure white with hard shadows

✗ Over-saturated, HDR “food porn” styling

✗ AI-generated humans, hands with wrong fingers, fake businesses
